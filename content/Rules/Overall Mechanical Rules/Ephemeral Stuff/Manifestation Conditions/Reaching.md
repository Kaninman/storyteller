**Description:** The spirit has opened a conduit through the Gauntlet, allowing it to use Influences and Numina to affect the other side. Numina with (R) after their name can be used with this Condition. Characters capable of perceiving spirits in Twilight can sense the conduit’s presence with a successful Wits + Composure roll.

**Causing the Condition:** This Condition is the result of the Reaching Manifestation Effect, and lasts for one Scene.

**Ending the Condition:** At the end of the scene, the Condition fades. Mortals may attempt an abjuration with a three-die penalty to close the conduit and end the Condition early.
