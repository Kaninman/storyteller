# Quickdraw (•)
***Prerequisites:** Wits •••, a Specialty in the weapon or fighting style chosen*

Effect: Choose a Specialty in Weaponry or Firearms when you purchase this Merit. Your character has trained in that weapon or style enough that pulling the weapon is his first reflex. Drawing or holstering that weapon is considered a reflexive action, and can be done any time his Defense applies ^merit
