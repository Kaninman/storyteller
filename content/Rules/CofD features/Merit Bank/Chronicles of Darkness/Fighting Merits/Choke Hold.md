# Choke Hold

**\*Prerequisite:** Brawl 2\*
If you can get your hands on someone, they’re putty in your hands. When grappling, your character can use the Choke move:

• Choke: If you rolled more successes than twice the victim’s Stamina, he’s unconscious for (six - Stamina) minutes. You must first have succeeded at a Hold move. If you don’t score enough successes at first, you can Choke on future turns and total your successes. ^choke
