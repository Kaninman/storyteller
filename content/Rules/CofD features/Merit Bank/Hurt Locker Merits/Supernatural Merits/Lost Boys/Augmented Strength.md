# Augmented Strength
***Prerequisites:** [[The Protocol]] Equal Level*

Effect: Your character’s been outfitted with stronger musculature, and more efficient arteries. For each dot in this Merit, add one dot to your character’s Strength. This affects all derived traits. This can take your character above her normal Attribute limit.

Deprived: You may take one lethal damage to add an additional dot of Strength for the scene. This may only be done once per scene.
