# Augmented Resilience (• to •••)
***Prerequisite:** [[The Protocol]] of equal level or higher*
Effect: Your character’s tissue has been enhanced for durability. For each dot in this Merit, add one dot to your character’s Stamina. This affects all derived traits. This can take your character above her normal Attribute limits.

Deprived: You also gain 2/1 armor
