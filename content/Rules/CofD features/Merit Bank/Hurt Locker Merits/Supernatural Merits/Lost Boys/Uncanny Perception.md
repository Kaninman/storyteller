Prerequisites: Protocol: Equal Level

Effect: Your character’s senses have been honed to alarm-
ing amounts. Add your Merit dots to all Perception-based
rolls. Additionally, add these Merit dots to any aimed attack
rolls your character makes.

Deprived: Double the given advantages. However, any strong
stimuli such as air horns or spotlights cause one point of lethal
damage to your character and destroy that sense for the scene.
