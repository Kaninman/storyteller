The entity may torture its victim’s mind via psychic assault. The Numen costs 1 Essence, and the activation roll is contested by the victim’s Intelligence + Supernatural Tolerance. If the entity succeeds, the victim suffers the [[Insane|Insane Tilt]] (p. 285) for the rest of the Scene.
