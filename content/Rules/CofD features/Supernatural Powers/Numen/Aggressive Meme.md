The entity speaks to a person (it must be in a Condition capable of doing so), and plants an idea in their mind. When that person tells someone else the idea, it takes hold in their mind, too, as well as in the minds of whomever they tell.

The Numen costs 7 Essence to activate, and is contested by Resolve + Composure + Supernatural Tolerance.
