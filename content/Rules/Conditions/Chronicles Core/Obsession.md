Something’s on your character’s mind, and she just can’t shake it. If she’s abiding by that obsession, exploring that emotion, or otherwise pursuing the object of the obsession, enjoy the 10-again quality on all rolls. When she’s not, you lose the 10-again quality.

**Resolution:** Fulfilling the obsession, therapy.
