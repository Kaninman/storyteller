Something has frightened your character extensively. Any time your character is taking an action where that fear might hinder her, you may opt to fail the roll (before you’ve made the roll), and shed this Condition. This Condition can be imposed by a successful breaking point roll. 

**Example Skills:** Brawl, Firearms, Intimidation, Weaponry

**Resolution:** Fail a roll, as noted.
