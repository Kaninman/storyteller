Your character has made inroads with a specified group. While she has this Condition, she gets a +2 to all rolls relating to that group. Alternately, she can shed this Condition to gain a one-time +3 bonus to influence or otherwise work with the group.

**Example Skills:** Politics, Socialize

**Resolution:** Condition shed, bridge burned.
