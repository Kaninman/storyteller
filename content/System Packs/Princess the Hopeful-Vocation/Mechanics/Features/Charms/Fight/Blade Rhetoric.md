# Mechanics

> Action: Reflexive
> Dice pool: Unrolled
> Cost: 1 Wisp
> Duration: 1 scene
> ^action

A Noble refuses to give up on others even as she comes to blows with them. This Charm can be activated only during combat. Until the Charm ends, the Noble can perform Social actions and Wits + Empathy actions reflexively, though only one at a time, and such actions may still take multiple turns each to complete depending
on how much she and her opponents say to each other. If she attempts to open a Door in social maneuvering
with such an action, she treats a hostile impression as average.
