The Noble’s weapon can cut distant foes. She can throw her weapon, treating it as an aerodynamic missile of Size 1 (regardless of its real size) and substituting Weaponry for Athletics in her dice pool and range calculation.

Alternately, she may spend a Wisp reflexively to attack a target up to 30 yards away with her weapon, using its normal dice pool - her swing or thrust sends a burst of energy at her target. Other effects of this Charm still apply to such attacks.
