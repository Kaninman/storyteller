The Noble’s weapon may move slowly, but it can’t be stopped or avoided. The Noble may transform her weapon, taking a -2 Initiative penalty. In return, those she attacks with it use only their Attributes as Defense from it; they don’t add any Skill. She may switch between this form and the basic form with a transformation action.
