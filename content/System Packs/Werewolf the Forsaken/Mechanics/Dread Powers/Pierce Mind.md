# Pierce Mind

**_Prerequisites: None_**
The creature can see minds as bright as lanterns. Like a lantern, it can peer close and see what is illuminated — or snuff a mind out. By spending 1 Essence and succeeding at a Presence + Resolve roll contested by the prey’s Resolve +
[[Supernatural Tolerance]], the creature can either read the target’s current thoughts for the remainder of the scene, sift through their memories for turns equal to successes rolled, or inflict bashing damage equal to the successes rolled.
