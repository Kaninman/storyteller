This Facet grants power over wave and water, over rain
and river. At the Uratha’s call, it surges, strengthens or recedes.
Cost: Varies
The Uratha gains Influence (Water) equal to her dots
in Wisdom. She can use this Influence in the same way as a
spirit. Her dice pool for Influence (Water) rolls is equal to her
Manipulation + Occult + Wisdom.
