Visibility here is poor and might only exist in glimpses and flashes, making it extremely hard to track movements and see details.

Affected characters suffer a -2 penalty to all vision-based rolls, including ranged combat. This increases to -3 at medium range and -4 at long range.

**Cause:** This Tilt applies to areas of low ambient light like the outdoors at night when there isn’t a new moon, disorienting areas like a dark nightclub with flashing strobes, specific areas beset by a vision-impairing phenomenon like a driving rainstorm or cloud of birds from Raven Trigger, or someplace under the effect of Obscuring Illusion. 

**Ending:** Bring a light source, get shelter from the vision-impairing phenomenon, or overcome the Obscuring Illusion, possibly by doing one of the above.
