Pain hurts! Your body’s perception of pain has been magnified, whether physically or magically, making injuries more debilitating. For each stack of this Tilt, your last Health box that would not cause a wound penalty now confers a wound penalty of -1, and all other wound penalties are made one point worse.

**Cause:** You were subject to a magical effect that increases the pain you suffer, like Agony of Defeat or Painful Illusion, or a mundane attack designed to increase pain, like a weapon covered in salt.

**Ending:** When none of your Health boxes are filled with
damage, the Tilt ends early.
