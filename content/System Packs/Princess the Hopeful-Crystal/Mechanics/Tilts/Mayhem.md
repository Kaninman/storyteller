There’s enemies all over! Hit them before they hit you!

You regard everyone in your vicinity as an immediate threat and must use your turn to attack one of them if possible, and flee if not.

**Cause:** A Horror used the Incite [[Mayhem]] Dread Power on you.

**Ending:** This Tilt lasts until you succeed on a Resolve + Composure roll penalized by the successes from the effect that caused this Tilt. Being restrained or calmed down allows you to reroll, and being startled with something new forces you to reroll.
