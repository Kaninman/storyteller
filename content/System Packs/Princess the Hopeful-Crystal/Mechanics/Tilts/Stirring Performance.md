Your feelings are surging in one direction or another. This Tilt came with a specific emotion. You have +2 on all dice pools for actions that align with that emotion.

**Cause:** You are the audience for the Anthem Charm.

**Ending:** The effect is suspended once a number of turns elapse from the turn Anthem was cast, depending on the choices made when it was cast. It starts up again if the caster spends another turn performing. At the end of the scene, it vanishes.
