The area is swarmed with a cloud of tiny creatures, like bees, locusts, or birds. They flap or buzz everywhere, attacking anything in the area.

A Swarm deals one bashing damage per round to everyone in its radius. This damage can increase if the Swarm contracts to focus on one spot: every time the Swarm halves its radius, the damage per round increases by 1. A swarm with a radius of 16 meters can deal 2 per round if it contracts to 8, or 3 per round if it contracts to 4, and so on. Armor protects against this damage only if it covers the whole body, and even then only applies half its value.

Anyone inside the Swarm, even if they’re not being attacked, gets -2 to all rolls from the distraction of being constantly battered by little creatures.

**Cause:** A supernatural power like Raven Trigger or a Dread Power, or a mundane event like kicking a hornet’s nest that makes a lot of small creatures very angry.

**Ending:** The swarm can’t be attacked by normal weapons, but effects with an area of effect or sources of flame can do the job. Each point of damage inflicted halves the Swarm’s radius, and once it’s below a 1 meter radius it disperses.
