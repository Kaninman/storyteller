Your next action that requires a die roll is -2 for each stack of this Tilt, unless that action is a direct attack against someone who inflicted this Tilt on you. This won’t affect rolls to contest abilities used on you, and if you’re under a Challenged effect from multiple people, you can attack any one of them without penalty.

**Cause:** Someone used the Duel Challenge Upgrade to the Kiai Charm on you.

**Ending:** Once you take an action that requires a die roll, whether it was an attack or not, all stacks of this Tilt expire
