It is already difficult to piece together that the two identities of a Princess are the same person when they seem to look similar. Veiling represents more extensive changes between mundane and Transformed state, like an entirely different facial structure or build. For each dot in Veiling, rolls to deduce the Princess’s identity are -1, and the number of Clues required to piece it together is increased by 1.
