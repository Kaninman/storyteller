Your character’s extremely good at finding her way into and out of [[Campaign- City of Sin/Locations/The Dreamlands & Dark World/The Dreamlands]]. She gets +2 on all rolls related to crossing through a Crawlspace, and when inside the Dreamlands, she can move the “exit portal” out of a given Crawlspace along with her, so that the next time it’s used it will deposit her at the new location.
