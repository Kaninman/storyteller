Anything is worth the sacrifice to fight the Darkness, and that starts with yourself. You know how to hurt yourself deeply to call out more power when needed—tearing yourself up inside becomes second nature.

Any time you take bashing or lethal damage from any source, including yourself, you may choose to make any amount of that damage Resistant and unable to be healed by magical means. If you do, immediately gain 1 Wisp for each bashing damage and 3 Wisps for each lethal damage you turned Resistant. When that damage heals naturally, you lose that many Wisps again—if you still have that many, that is. Spend them before you heal. 

This Merit is granted freely at creation to Princesses of Storms. If the Princess is a current follower of the Queen of Storms, it is always active. Other Princesses can use this Merit if they have 3 or more dots of the Tempesta Signet
