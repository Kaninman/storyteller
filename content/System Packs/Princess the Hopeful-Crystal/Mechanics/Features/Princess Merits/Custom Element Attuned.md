You have an affinity for magic that works on or through a specific element, particular to you. Treat this as an Elemental-Attuned Signet (p. 137), except you designate an element instead of the Signet, and instead of using an associated Signet’s dots you use your non-Flared Inner Light.

The element you choose should be about as limiting as the ones already provided by Radiant signets. It doesn’t have to be traditional—if you can figure out how to make something like “math” or “boundaries” a meaningful but usable restriction, you can go with that—but it can be a traditional element like lightning, metal, or spirit as well.

If you are a Princess of Currents, and the element you choose is lightning/electricity, this Merit only costs •• and you may use your Acqua Signet dots.
