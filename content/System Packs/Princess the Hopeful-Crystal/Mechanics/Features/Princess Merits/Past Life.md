A great deal of Princess characters won’t be able to access Professional Training—after all, if you’re 16 years old, how much on the job training have you really had? However a Princess could have had lifetimes of experience in the Fearless Kingdoms. Most people don’t carry over much from their past lives ( it’s kind of like a New Game + thing), but a Princess in tune with her Past Life is able to recall bits and pieces and draw on them for expertise.

When you take the Past Life Merit, choose a role you had in a previous incarnation and two important Skills for it (use the chart on CofD p.50 for inspiration, or make your own) to be your Asset Skills. The benefits of Past Life and Professional Training do not stack: an Asset Skill for one is not the same as an Asset Skill for the other, and a Skill that is an Asset Skill for both only gains the benefit of one or the other (so don’t take both, it will just confuse you). If your character has a profession that deeply ties into their Past Life, you can choose to take more dots in Past Life to represent knowledge in that profession instead of increased affinity with a previous incarnation, so you don’t have to
have a weird melange of two Merits for the same Skills.

- • **Distant Memories:** You remember this. Or you remember that you used to remember this, and expertise wants to come to your hands. When you spend a Willpower on a mundane roll with an Asset Skill, you also gain 8-again.
- •• **Wait, I Should Know This:** You can call upon memories of your past life to help avoid mistakes you knew you made then. After making a mundane roll with an Asset Skill, you may spend a Willpower to reroll it once. You must take the second result, even if it’s worse.
- ••• **Confident Competence:** The breadth of experience you had is coming back to you. Choose another Skill that was part of your previous role, and add it to your Asset Skills. Gain two Specialties in your Asset Skills (two total, not two each).
- •••• **Like Riding A Bicycle:** This is all second nature—you’re in tune with your past life and the knowledge you had and the lessons you learned flow naturally from and into your perfected self. Gain a Luminous Beat for every mundane dot you have in your Asset Skills. When you buy or upgrade to a mundane dot in an Asset Skill, gain a Luminous Beat.
- ••••• **Surpassing the Past:** You’re better than you were in your past life by now—you’ve merged that experience with your own to figure out things neither life could accomplish individually. When you make a mundane roll with an Asset Skill, you may spend a Wisp to make that roll a rote action
