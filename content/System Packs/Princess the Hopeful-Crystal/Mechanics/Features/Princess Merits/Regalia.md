The Regalia Merit allows the Princess to incorporate equipment into her Regalia that manifests when she Transforms. This makes it always reflexively available while Transformed, allows her to resummon, recreate, or dismiss it as necessary, and allows it to provide equipment bonuses to Charms.

Detailed rules for the creation of Regalia are found in Appendix I (p. 262). If you don’t want to use the detailed rules, then one dot of Regalia buys one Availability worth of equipment (this is actually a worse return on investment than Resources, which can buy multiple pieces of equipment, but you can take it anywhere and never lose it). Equipment that provides a skill bonus provides that bonus to a Charm using the same skill.

Whatever the mechanical traits of your Regalia, it takes whatever physical form you want without changing how it works.For example, you can buy a pair of night vision goggles as Regalia, decide that they look like red and blue cellophane 3D glasses, and when you Transform you will have a pair of red and blue cellophane 3D glasses that provide all the abilities and benefits of night vision goggles. Whatever form your Regalia takes is for self-expression, not concealment; whatever you use will obviously be providing some effect.
