When you spend a Wisp for Defiance to protect yourself from incoming damage that would upgrade bashing to lethal or lethal to aggravated damage, prevent all but one of that damage. You can’t then prevent that one damage with another use of Defiance.

Defiance makes it clear that your magic is repelling some of the damage, but if you fall over while using this ability, you can make it look like your magic tried and failed to protect you from a hit that would knock you out.
