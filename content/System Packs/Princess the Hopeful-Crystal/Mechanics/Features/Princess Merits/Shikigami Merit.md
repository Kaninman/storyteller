You are aided by a shikigami, a delegate from the Dreamlands sent by the Queens to help your nakama in your mission. Shikigami are able to channel the abilities of the Princesses they aid and are very useful for supporting your efforts.

This Merit is granted freely at creation to all Radiant Princesses. As long as a Princess is a member in good standing of a Radiant nakama and has the shikigami’s trust, it will still be active even if they are Twilight or Queenless.

Rules for building and using [[Shikigami Rules|Shikigami]]are found on p.251.
