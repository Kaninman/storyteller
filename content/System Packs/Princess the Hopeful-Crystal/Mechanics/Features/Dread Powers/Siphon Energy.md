# Siphon Energy

This Horror is able to directly drain the life and Light from its victims, empowering itself by sucking their Willpower.

At one dot, the Horror can drain one Willpower per turn from humans it is physically touching, and add it to their own. At two dots, the Horror can grant this power to its subordinates, and also siphon energy from them without touching them.
