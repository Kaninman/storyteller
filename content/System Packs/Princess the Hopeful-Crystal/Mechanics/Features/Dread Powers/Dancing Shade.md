This Horror’s mastery over darkness and shadow is so great that its literal shadow serves it. The one-dot version of this Power allows the Horror to spend a Willpower and take its action on its turn via its Shadow. The Shadow moves along walls and surfaces and is able to interact with other objects by their shadows, even if the real physical objects are nowhere near each other; this bypasses Defense and most other ways of keeping something away because it’s very hard to dodge in two dimensions when one of them is “up”. 

The two-dot version of this Power allows the Horror to separate its Shadow to act independently by spending 3 Willpower. The Shadow is a Simplified Horror with 5 dice in whatever shadow-specific task it is charged with, Worst At interacting with things that can’t be done by interacting with shadows, Potency equal to its creator, 3 Defense, and 5 Health. It is damaged only by magic and direct illumination, which is treated as if it dealt fire damage.
