Most commonly used by Defilers, a Horror with this Dread Power can directly strengthen the hold of the All-Consuming Darkness on an area, and its grasp of fear on those who live in it. 

This is an extended action with an interval of 1 day. The Horror has to shore up the borders of its desired “fortress” with black magic and icons of fear with each roll; once it has accumulated 7 successes, the area suffers an additional stack of the All-Consuming Darkness Condition (p. 274). This can be magically dispelled, but all 7 successes need to be negated or dealt with to clear the whole area; smaller subsections require proportionately less. Thus a smaller Fortress of Terror is harder to make a foothold in, because its owner will be more likely to see you and stop you.
