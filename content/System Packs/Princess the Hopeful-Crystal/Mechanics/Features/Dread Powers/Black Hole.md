The Horror creates something that draws in all attacks, attention, matter, even air. By spending 2 Willpower, the Horror conjures an object that re-orients gravity toward it in the immediate area. Everyone nearby (save the Horror or targets it designates) as well as unsecured objects, will “fall” toward the Black Hole unless they can hold on to something heavy or anchored. Anything in the Black Hole takes lethal damage equal to its activation successes every turn. The Black Hole lasts as long as the Horror concentrates on maintaining it, and it vanishes if the Horror takes any action other than moving or takes a point of lethal damage.
