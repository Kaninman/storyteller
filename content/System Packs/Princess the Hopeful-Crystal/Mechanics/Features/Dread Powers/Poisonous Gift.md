The Darkness isn’t patient, but it does like to spread. Sometimes a slow spread is worth the wait. This Dread Power does nothing on its own and is used in tandem with other Dread Powers that require the user to touch the victim or be in their proximity. The Horror can spend a Willpower to invest their Dread Power into an object (paying all costs for it as normal). If someone gets a full night’s sleep near the object, they are subject to the Dread Power as if they had been touched for long enough to apply the power once.
