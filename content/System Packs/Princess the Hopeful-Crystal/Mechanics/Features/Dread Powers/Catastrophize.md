The Horror can spend a Willpower to amp up the emotions of a victim, contesting against their Composure + Potency. If successful, they inflict the Guilty condition on them. This typically concerns an act they have taken that they wouldn’t normally have cause to feel Guilty over, convincing them it’s actually evidence they’re a horrible person. No communication is required to use this power, but communication might be required to find out something they did to hook them for.
