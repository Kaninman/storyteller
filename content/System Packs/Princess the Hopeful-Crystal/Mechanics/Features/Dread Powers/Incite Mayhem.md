Some fear gnaws constantly, wearing down the soul. And some says “HOLY SHIT THAT DUDE IS ABOUT TO KILL YOU!” Incite Mayhem inflames the latter. By spending 1 Willpower, the Horror inflicts the Mayhem tilt on an individual it can sense. They must succeed on a Resolve + Composure roll penalized by the Horror’s successes, or regard everyone around them as an immediate threat and take an action to attack. Once someone has succeeded on this roll, they don’t need to roll again until the Dread Power is used again, or something significantly surprises or startles them.

At two dots, the Horror can spend 3 Willpower to inflict Mayhem on everyone in the area.
