The Horror embodies the ever-growing Darkness, sowing it into the real world and sucking in a rich harvest it gathers. Once per scene, the Horror can spend 1 Willpower to perform a terrifying display. This potentially intimidates everyone who can perceive it, contesting their Resolve + Potency. Success inflicts the Beaten Down tilt on them. The Horror regains one Willpower whenever someone fails a roll to resist the fear it inspires (even if that fear was not prompted by the specific use of this Power.)
