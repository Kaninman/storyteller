Some claim that Horrors are so foul that the weak human mind erases them from memory rather than deal with their existence. Whether or not that’s true, some Horrors cheat to do this anyway.

At the end of a scene including a Horror with this Dread Power, the Horror may spend 3 Willpower to attempt to erase itself from the episodic memory of everyone who saw it, contesting against their Resolve + Potency. This activation has a bonus or penalty between +3 and -3 depending on how noticeable and major the events were. If the Horror succeeds, the victims do not directly remember the Horror and lose their memories of events that happened during the scene. They retain all emotional associations with the memories hidden from them, but may need to rationalize other explanations for the outcome of the scene.
