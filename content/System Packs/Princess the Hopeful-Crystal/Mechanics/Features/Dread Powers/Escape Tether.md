This Horror has a means of retreat to the Dark World when the chips are down, allowing it to escape and spread fear another day. This Horror is attuned to a safe site (well, for certain values of “safe”) in the Dark World to which it can return. It takes one Willpower and an entire turn to activate this ability. This is disrupted if the Horror is struck in melee or otherwise smacked around. If it goes off successfully, the Horror transports back to the Dark World, but always leaves a clue as to where it retreated to. If confronted in that Dark World safe site, the ability won’t be very useful; it’s impossible to tether to more than one location.
