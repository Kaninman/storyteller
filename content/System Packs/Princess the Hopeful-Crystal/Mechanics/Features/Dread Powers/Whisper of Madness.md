The Horror is capable of inserting messages directly into its victim’s thoughts. They come with no compulsion to act on them—they can seem like intrusive thoughts or even auditory hallucinations—but if similar enough to the victim’s normal internal narrative, they seem like sudden realizations or observations. The Horror can attempt to insert a single sentence into the victim’s inner monologue at no cost. It may spend a Willpower to fabricate a Perception roll for the victim (contested appropriately), who then has an experience as if they had made that roll and seen the result the Horror decided to show. This allows for Social Maneuvering rolls over time without the target being aware they are being Maneuvered.
