This Horror can link its essence to someone imprisoned by fear, and draw power from them. If the Horror doesn’t already have Pure Darkness, it can gain the Pure Darkness Dread Power by attuning itself to a Subverted Nightscape: it gets the Pure Darkness bonus based on its own Potency or the Potency of the Nightscape, whichever is lower.

If the Horror already has Pure Darkness, the Nightscape doesn’t need to be Subverted, and they always use the better of their own Potency and the Nightscape’s Potency to determine their bonuses.
