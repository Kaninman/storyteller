This Horror cannot create flawless illusions that fool every sense. Rather, they create something flawed and then cow their victims into overlooking the flaws. This Dread Power is used in two stages. By spending a Willpower, the Horror may apply Coercive Illusion to an existing object. Then, they must succeed at intimidating the victim in order for them to see the object as a specific and similar thing they would be afraid of seeing in this context. By setting up a situation where the thing they need would be fearful in context, the Horror can fabricate all sorts of images.
