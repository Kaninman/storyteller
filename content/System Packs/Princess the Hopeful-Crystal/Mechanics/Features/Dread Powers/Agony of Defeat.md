The presence of a Horror is already painful in a physical and a metaphorical sense, but this Horror takes it further. The wounds it inflicts are deep and agonizing. At 1 dot, the Horror reflexively attempts to inflict [[Heightened Pain]] (p. 270) on any target it deals damage to; the target must succeed on a Stamina roll or suffer one stack of the Tilt. At 2 dots, the Horror’s very presence inflicts pain: each character in proximity to it takes one phantom bashing damage per turn, which immediately activates the 1-dot ability of this Power.
