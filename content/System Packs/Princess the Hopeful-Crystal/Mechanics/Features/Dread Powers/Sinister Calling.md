Aspiration is the essence of the Light, the ability to hope and dream of a brighter future and act to make it happen. Aspirations are core to any character in the Chronicles of Darkness. Of course the Darkness wants to twist them.

This Horror may spend a Willpower to engage in an extended, contested roll against its victim’s Integrity. The target number of this roll is the current amount of Willpower points the victim has, not their Willpower dots. If successful, the victim gains the Sinister Calling Condition, which twists one of their Aspirations into a dark version of the Horror’s design. If the Horror doesn’t know any of its victim’s Aspirations, however, this does nothing
