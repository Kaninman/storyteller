With a growl, an occult incantation, a high-pitched shriek, or an extended finger and a shout of “FUCK HIM UP!” the Horror is able to direct Darkspawn to do their bidding. Any Horror with this power appears to Darkspawn as if they possess no Light whatsoever and are not a valid target for predation, though their actions may cause the creature to conclude they are a threat anyway.

This Horror can issue a command to all of one specific type of a Darkspawn in the area. If the command is for something the Darkspawn generally wanted to do anyway, it takes no roll and requires no Willpower—that Calamitous wanted to destroy something and is more than happy to destroy the specific place you picked out for it. If the command requires restraint or strategy outside the Darkspawn’s normal behavior, the Horror must spend a Willpower and match or exceed the Darkspawn’s Potency on an appropriate activation roll for the command to be followed.

Command Darkspawn only works on “lesser” embodiments of Pure Darkness. All Darkspawn are subject to it, but Cataphracts, Enthroned, and Defilers aren’t. Other creatures made with the Horror rules may or may not be considered “lesser” creatures of the All-Consuming Darkness at the Storyteller’s discretion, even if they don’t have the Pure Darkness Dread Power. Defilers and

Cataphractoi get this Dread Power for free.
