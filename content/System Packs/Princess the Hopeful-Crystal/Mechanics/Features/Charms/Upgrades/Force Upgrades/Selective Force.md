The counterpart to Elemental Force. When you create your locus, designate a number of specific people or objects you can perceive; the locus only acts on/against those people or objects. Everything else is completely intangible to the locus, meaning there is no penalty or opposition or difficulty from interacting with any other object or material (but you have to deal with their mass and resistance like normal).
