Gifted to Princesses of Mirrors—allows Manipulation to be used instead of Strength
You move in sync with your magical force. You no longer have to concentrate to direct your locus, so long as what you do with your locus is part of the same action you’re performing (and the locus is capable of the action). Among other benefits, this will usually allow you to get a Teamwork bonus for actions at the very least
