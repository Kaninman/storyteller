Your locus now encompasses a larger area. The base Size of your locus is now twice your Inner Light, and each -1 penalty you take to your casting roll increases the Size by an amount equal to your Inner Light. Your locus can manipulate freely in that area without needing to be moved and may interact with multiple distant things at once as part of the same action.
