Your Force now spreads deeper than the realms of the physical alone. Your locus and its ability to manipulate extend into Twilight and act against other, non-physical phenomena. If you can create or infuse physical phenomena into your locus, now you can do the same with metaphysical properties. If you can block passage of physical objects, you can now intercept spells and magical effects, and so on. You still need to know the kind of effects you intend to interact with.
