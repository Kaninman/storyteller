This is an advanced Upgrade. It counts as two Upgrades when applied to a Charm.
When you make your locus, you can summon an additional locus for each time you apply this Upgrade. You may apply this Upgrade multiple times, up to a maximum of your Inner Light, but it costs an extra Upgrade slot for each one.

You may direct multiple loci at once to all manipulate together—you don’t have to do this for all the loci if you have some that are already doing what you want with Enduring Force, but you can’t give two directions in a single turn. If this requires a roll, consider it a teamwork action where one locus is the primary actor and the others all assist.
