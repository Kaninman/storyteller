Your locus is capable of manipulating the area by becoming entirely solid: providing a stepping stone, or stopping objects and attacks that would pass through it. It has a Structure equal to your casting successes. It acts as Concealment (CofD p. 90) of appropriate size, and any damage dealt through a Barrier-protected target depletes the Barrier’s Structure first. A Barrier stays active even when not concentrated on, but if you spend your turn to concentrate, it recovers all Structure and gains Durability equal to half your Inner Light for that turn.
