An ounce of prevention is worth a pound of cure, and all that. You can mend to fortify against injury in the future, bolstering defenses physical and spiritual.

If you choose a specific type of injury you could treat but the patient or object doesn’t have right now, then they gain the Vaccinated Condition (p. 128), which will activate Restoration on them when they suffer that injury.

You have to be somewhat specific when naming the in- jury. You can say “bullet wounds,” but you can’t say “lethal damage,” and you don’t have to be as specific as “.357 magnum hollow-points.” They can already have the injury in the case of Upgrades like Invigorating Restoration or Arresting Restoration, in which case you put a time limit on it.

You can also choose to make the patient or object develop resistance to your own treatment. If you do, then when you mend an injury, set a condition or time limit on it once the condition is met or the time expires, the injury reappears.

Either way, the Vaccinated Condition only lasts 12 hours per Inner Light; after that, it fades without activating.
