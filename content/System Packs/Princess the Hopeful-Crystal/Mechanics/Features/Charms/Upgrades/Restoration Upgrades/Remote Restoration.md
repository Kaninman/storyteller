You don’t need to touch something to mend it. You now mend by “focusing healing energy into one or more targets you can directly perceive.” If you attempt to mend an uncooperative patient, subtract their Defense from your dice pool as if it were a thrown weapon. (Restoration, Stat! lets you ignore their Defense if they don’t have an effect that allows them to retain their Defense against firearms.)

You can designate additional targets for your Restoration by paying 1 Wisp for each, up to a total no more than your Inner Light. If you do, you have to treat the same Injury on all of them, and if you use an Upgrade that makes every roll cost a Wisp, you don’t have to pay the extra Wisps on subsequent rolls.
