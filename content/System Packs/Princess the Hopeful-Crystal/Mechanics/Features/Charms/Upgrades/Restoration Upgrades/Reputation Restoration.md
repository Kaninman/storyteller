Gifted to Princesses of Mirrors—allows Manipulation to be used instead of Intelligence

Sometimes it’s not enough to just heal what is internal to the patient. Sometimes you gotta heal how people see them.

The injury you treat may now be “a Social Condition or Tilt, or blocked access to one or more Social Merits.” To remove a Condition or Tilt, the required successes are dependent on the Condition, with more complex and long-lasting Conditions taking more successes and Tilts only taking one or two. The interval between rolls extends to 24 hours, each roll costs a Wisp, and you can interrupt and resume your cast as many times as you want. You must also interact with other people who create the Social Condition, even if you don’t have to do all the things you’d normally do to patch up the Condition without magic.

If you finish treating a Persistent Condition, and it wasn’t applied in the current scene, the patient has to sacrifice a Willpower dot to avoid it coming back after one day per Inner Light of the caster.
