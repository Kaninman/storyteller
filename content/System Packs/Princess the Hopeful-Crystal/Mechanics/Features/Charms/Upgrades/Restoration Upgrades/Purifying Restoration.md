Some injury comes and goes – you get stabbed, and now you have a stab wound, and the knife isn’t really relevant any more. Sometimes, it’s a bit more sticky and pervasive. The injury you treat may now be “the presence of foreign substances or contaminants,” with the required successes dependent on the nature of the contaminant and how pervasively it is integrated. Not only can this cure poisons and diseases, but it can also purify substances—you could make any liquid into pure drinking water, clean the blood off your clothes, or turn a lump of bronze into a lump of copper and a lump of tin, if for some reason you needed to do that.
