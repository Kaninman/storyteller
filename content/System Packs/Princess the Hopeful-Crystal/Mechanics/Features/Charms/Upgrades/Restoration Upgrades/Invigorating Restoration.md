You can mend people and things to tip top shape, the most optimal they’ve been, beating back stress and exhaustion.

The injury you treat may now be “this object doesn’t have a high enough Durability” or “this patient has an Attribute that is lower than mine.” To add Durability to an object, the first costs 1 success, the second costs 2, etc.

Used on a patient, you add to their Attribute dots. The first dot costs 1 success, the second costs 2, etc. You cannot raise any Attribute to a level higher than your own rating in that Attribute (counting your Transformed Attribute dots, but not counting buffs from any other source). Invigorating Restoration lasts for a scene. Casting it multiple times in a scene doesn’t reset the cost increase for each Attribute or Durability.
