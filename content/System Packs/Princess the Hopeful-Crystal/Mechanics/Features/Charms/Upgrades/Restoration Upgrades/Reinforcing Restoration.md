Gifted to Princesses of Swords—allows Medicine to be used instead of Crafts

Some injuries last longer and run deeper than the things modeled by damage mechanics. You need to put people back together, sometimes.

The injury you treat may now be “A physical Condition or Tilt.” To remove a Condition or Tilt, the required successes are dependent on the Condition, with more complex and long-lasting Conditions taking more successes and Tilts or very recent Conditions only taking one or two. Unless the Condition you are treating was added in the current scene, the interval between rolls extends to one day, each roll costs a Wisp, and you can interrupt and resume your cast as many times as you want.

If you finish treating a Persistent Condition, and it wasn’t applied in the current scene, the patient has to sacrifice a Willpower dot to avoid it coming back after one day per Inner Light of the caster.
