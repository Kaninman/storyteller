Gifted to Princesses of Currents—allows Crafts to be used instead of Medicine

You can creatively bend, twist, and mend an object in order to restore it to a condition it was never in, even combining it with other objects. The injury you treat may now be “not having the Equipment Modifications I want.” Each success spent on this Upgrade provides a single point of Equipment Modifications (see the Regalia Appendix, p. 262) to the object and lowers its Structure by 1.
