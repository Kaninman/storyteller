Gifted to Princesses of Tears—allows Occult to be used instead of Crafts or Medicine

Some problems might take longer to solve than you have, and some problems need to be frozen to keep them from getting worse.

The injury you treat may now be “this patient or object isn’t in stasis,” mending it by putting it into stasis. The required successes are equal to the patient or object’s Size. A patient or object in Stasis does not move or act, does not experience the passage of time, and is immune to any action taken against it other than to physically move its location or dispel the Stasis effect. Stasis lasts for one scene, but can be extended by spending two more Wisps for each day you want the effect to continue.

An unwilling and living patient is entitled to repeat their Stamina + Resolve roll any time something new and potentially threatening happens, which includes every turn in an active combat situation.
