The injury you treat may now be the existence of an ongoing magical effect. This always triggers a Clash of Wills against the original caster; effects innate to something’s nature aren’t able to be removed this way. If the injury is currently suppressed by the Ward Charm, add the successes of that Charm to the successes of your roll in the Clash of Wills.
