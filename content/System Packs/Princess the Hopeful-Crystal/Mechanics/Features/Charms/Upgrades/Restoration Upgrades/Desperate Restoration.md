This is an advanced Upgrade. It counts as two Upgrades when applied to a Charm.

You can now mend the most dire wounds. You may directly mend aggravated damage for 1 success per point, instead of having to downgrade it to lethal for 2 points. You can even mend the last health box of aggravated damage that killed a character, so long as they suffered that injury in the current scene—so you can raise the dead if you get there right away. If they were brought back to life, the patient automatically loses a dot of Integrity, Belief, or its equivalent. Being truly dead, even for a few seconds, is an experience that shakes you to the core.
