Gifted to Princesses of Storms

Nothing stands in the way of your goal. When you move in a straight line during Step, everyone and everything within 1 yard per Inner Light of your path takes 1 lethal damage (ignoring Durability) and must make a Stamina + Potency roll or be shoved that many meters away from you. Inanimate objects are thrown out of your way as if they were pushed by someone with Strength equal to your Inner Light, regardless if they are externally braced or reinforced.

No person or thing can be damaged by this more than once per turn.
