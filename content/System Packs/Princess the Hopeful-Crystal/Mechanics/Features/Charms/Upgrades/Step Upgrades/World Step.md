Gifted to Princesses of Flowers

Where you leap, the ground meets—or what you meet, becomes the ground. During Step, you may orient your relative gravity to any surface you touch—you will stand on, run on, jump off of, and fall toward that surface. If this surface is a person or other entity that can act, they must make a grapple check opposed by your (Strength or Dexterity) + Athletics to break this Upgrade’s effect.
