Gravity has less of a hold on you. During Step, you selectively fall at one-tenth the normal speed of gravity, as does anything you interact with. You, or anyone or anything you bring with you, won’t be able to change trajectory in the air without something to kick off of or some other ability to alter your momentum, like Force.
