Gifted to Princesses of Currents

Your perception of time slows to a crawl; time only seems to move when you do. You get no penalties for being rushed or not having adequate time to react and can perform actions that would be impossible due to how quickly you would have to perceive movements or changes around you. Among other effects, this allows you to go first in any combat round unless there are other combatants present with supernatural initiative effects, like this Upgrade or a vampire’s Celerity; in that case everyone who goes first acts in order of their Initiative and then everyone else goes in order of their Initiative.
