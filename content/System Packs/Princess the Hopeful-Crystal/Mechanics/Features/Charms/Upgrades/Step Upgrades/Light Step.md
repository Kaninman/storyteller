Gifted to Princesses of Spades

During Step, you selectively weigh no more than a playing card as you need it, allowing you to perform astonishing feats of grace. It takes no conscious effort to determine what purposes you have your normal weight for; you can stand on the tip of someone’s sword because you weigh no more than a card, but you won’t be blown off their sword by the wind because you weigh 125 pounds. In addition to the other possibilities this opens up, feats of grace or agility that aren’t attacks gain the rote quality.
