Gifted to Princesses of Flowers—allows Composure to be used instead of Wits
Images normally only fool sight and sound, and can somewhat fool touch with Blunt Illusion, and those are normally all you need. But with Extensive Illusion, you get the full package. Your image can fool any and all senses, supernatural or mundane, and give out any false sensations you wish. Any attempt to see through the image has to overcome the successes of the Charm instead of a normal Clash of Wills, no matter what sense, divination, or magic is used.
