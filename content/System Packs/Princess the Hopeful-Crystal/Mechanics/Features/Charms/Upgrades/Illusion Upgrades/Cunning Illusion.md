Gifted to Princesses of Currents—allows Crafts to be used instead of Subterfuge

With Cunning Illusion, you do not need to focus to direct your image; you may have it carry out actions according to a script. The image is not intelligent, but runs off of your intelligence in the “background”; thus, the script you give it must be something you could carry out while distracted by music or dicking around on your phone. “If anyone comes by, blow them off and make an excuse” and “stomp around and make monster noises” are acceptable types of commands, while “explain to everyone that I have cancer and can’t come to class” or “follow that guy at a distance of 15 meters and look like you are trying to stay hidden but make sure he can see you” are not.
