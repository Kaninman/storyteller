Your image doesn’t have to be of your own design. You can flicker in something based on someone else’s thoughts or perceptions. If that target is unwilling, this is automatically contested by Resolve + Composure in addition to the Wits + Composure roll to see through the image. You cannot read the target’s mind and must specify the kind of image you will summon with somewhat loose criteria; you can specify “the guy you are waiting here to meet” or “the dinosaur you designed and asked me to make an image of” or “the annoying bullshit you knew, you just fucking knew you were going to have to deal with today.” You can’t use this to retrieve specific information like “your credit card number” or “the contents of the documents in your briefcase,” but nice try. The target gets a -2 dice penalty to see through the Illusion due to its plausibility.
