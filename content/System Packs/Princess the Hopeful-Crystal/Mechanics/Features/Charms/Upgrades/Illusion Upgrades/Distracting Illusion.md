Gifted to Princesses of Spades—allows Larceny to be used instead of Subterfuge

A Distracting Illusion grabs the attention of its viewers, drawing their focus to the exclusion of anything else. Up-close magic is all about misdirection. Viewers of your image must roll Wits + Composure, penalized by your Illusion successes, to notice anything else going on or to apply their Defense. Once they succeed on this roll once, or take damage from an attack they couldn’t apply their Defense against, they don’t need to roll again for the same image.
