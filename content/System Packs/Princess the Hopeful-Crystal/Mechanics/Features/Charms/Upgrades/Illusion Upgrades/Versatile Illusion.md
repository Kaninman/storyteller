You may now flicker another image while you have one active without dispelling it. It can be directed independently of the others and maintaining all the images does not cost any more focus or attention than one. When you flicker, the activation roll gains a -2 penalty for each existing image. On a successful activation roll, consider the successes rolled for all your simultaneous images to be the lowest roll you made for any of them; failing a roll causes all the images to dispel.
