Normally, you are aware of your own illusions, but they appear the same to everyone else unless they are dispelled. With this Upgrade, you can let anyone you choose in on the secret. An image made with Pandering Illusion will be either visible, translucent, or utterly imperceptible to any individual or group you specify.. You can’t specify criteria you aren’t personally aware of for Pandering Illusion, so you can’t say “only vampires can see this” and then root out vampires by seeing who flinches at the image, but Extensive Illusion can help you with that.
