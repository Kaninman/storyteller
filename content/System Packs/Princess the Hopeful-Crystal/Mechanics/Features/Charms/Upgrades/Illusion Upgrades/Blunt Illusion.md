Gifted to Princesses of Swords—allows Intelligence to be used instead of Wits

With Blunt Illusion, your images have mass and are no longer be dispelled by physical contact. They have no more strength than required to lift a single sheet of paper but can occupy space and aren’t revealed by indirect or glancing contact. Direct, forceful, and deliberate contact will still dispel the image.
