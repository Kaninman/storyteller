Gifted to Princesses of Storms—allows Dexterity to be used instead of
Wits

If an illusion can fool the senses, well, pain is a sense, isn’t it? A Painful Illusion is capable of dealing damage to living things by inflicting phantom pain. An
image may deal two damage per turn to everyone within it (if it’s something like an illusory flame) or may be used to execute attacks that deal damage normally. Use the Princess’s appropriate dice pool for any attack roll the image makes, not the dice pool for casting.

Damage inflicted by the image is Illusory: the damage is always Bashing and subsequent “real damage” will replace illusory damage instead of being added to it, but it still applies wound penalties, and anyone who suffers it must succeed on a Stamina roll or take a stack of the [[Heightened Pain]] Tilt (p. 270), which makes wound penalties start 1 point earlier and go one point higher.
