Gifted to Princesses of Mirrors—allows Manipulation to be used instead of Wits

Your Signature Illusion is something you always have close at hand, something that’s easy to recall. Choose a specific type of image or a few closely related ones to be your Signature; when you flicker that image, you do so reflexively instead of instantly. If you use Versatile Illusion, additional copies of your Signature Illusion do not cost any Wisps to create, but you still roll for them.
