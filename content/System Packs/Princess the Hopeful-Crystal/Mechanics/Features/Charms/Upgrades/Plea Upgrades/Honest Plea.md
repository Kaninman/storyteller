Gifted to Princesses of Jewels—allows Socialize to be used instead of Persuasion
Other creatures have the ability to lie and make it convincing, even compel belief. You don’t. You don’t need it. You speak from the heart, you bare the truth.

Your request may now include “and please make a Wits+Empathy roll to see if I am lying, where you gain au- tomatic bonus successes equal to my Inner Light.” This is not a supernatural compulsion upon them, it opens yourself to their ability to discern lies (and obviously doesn’t work if you’re lying).

Among its other effects, if their Wits + Empathy roll plus the number of successes required to open a Door from them is 10 or more, then improve Impression or Distance by 1. (This means it’s harder to get this benefit the fewer successes you require). If they were already at the maximum, this has no benefit.
