Your Buster damage repeats against anyone and anything it strikes. Each subsequent turn, the target must succeed on a reflexive (Resolve or Stamina) + Potency roll, penalized by the activation successes of the Buster hit, or take the Buster attack’s weapon damage again. This roll gets a stacking +2 bonus each round after the first.
