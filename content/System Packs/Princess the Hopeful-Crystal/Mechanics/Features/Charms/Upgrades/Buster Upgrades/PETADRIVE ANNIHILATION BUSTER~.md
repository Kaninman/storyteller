Gigadrive Exceed’s angry older sister who’s been sent to juvie multiple times. Petadrive Annihilation Buster grants +4 dice to your Buster, it can be applied multiple times, AND it isn’t restricted by how many Buster Overcharge
dice you have.

It has two drawbacks: One, you cannot use free Upgrades to apply Petadrive Annihilation—every application costs a Wisp. Second, you must spend every single Buster Overcharge die you have on it, which must be at least four.
