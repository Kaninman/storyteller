This is an advanced Upgrade. It counts as two Upgrades when applied to a Charm.

When your Buster targets any being with a Potency Trait, roll additional dice equal to that Trait. This can’t provide more dice than the total number of Buster Overcharge dice you spent. (The same Overcharge can be “counted” for Judgment Light and Gigadrive Exceed, so between the two Upgrades they can add two dice for each Overcharge you spend.)
