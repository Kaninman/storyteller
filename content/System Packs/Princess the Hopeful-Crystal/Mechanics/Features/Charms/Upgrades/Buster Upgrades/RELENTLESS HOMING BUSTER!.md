Your Buster can go around corners and over obstacles. You still have to know where your target is to within 2 meters—a spotter is very helpful—and they can still attempt to get out of the way of the attack when it’s coming, but you ignore line-of-sight, cover, and the target’s Defense if they have an effect that lets them apply it to ranged attacks.
