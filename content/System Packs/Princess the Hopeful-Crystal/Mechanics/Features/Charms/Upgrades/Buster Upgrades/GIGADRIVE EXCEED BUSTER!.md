This is the basic Buster ability, and everyone starts with it for good reason: it’s pretty much always useful. Gigadrive Exceed can be applied to a Buster multiple times, and each time you do, it increases the Buster’s dice pool by +2. The bonus dice given by Gigadrive Exceed can’t exceed the normal number of Buster Overcharge dice you rolled.
