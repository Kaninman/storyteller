This is an advanced Upgrade. It counts as two Upgrades when applied to a Charm.

The blistering, radiant energy of your Buster sears away magical effects in its path. When targeted at someone who has active magical effects that can be subject to a Clash of Wills, this Buster forces a Clash against one of them (either one of your choice, or one at random if you don’t know.)

Your successes on the Clash of Wills can’t exceed the successes you rolled for Buster. If you succeed, the magical effect is suppressed for the scene.
