When you cast Buster, don’t roll for it or consume your Buster Overcharge dice yet. Your Buster fires off on your next turn, taking your movement and action. If you took damage from a melee attack in the intervening time, the Buster fails. Otherwise, resolve the dice pool then, using your target’s current cover, concealment, range, etc, and allowing them to apply their Defense—but double your dice pool before subtracting any penalties.

This Upgrade can be applied multiple times, and each time you do, increase the number of turns you can charge it by 1, and each turn you charge it adds your base dice pool to the activation roll again
