You empower your Buster with occult magic and apply the supernatural Bane or Ban properties of one other kind of event or material to it, causing the Buster to serve as that Bane or Ban. Pick one kind of Bane when you buy this Up- grade that you may always apply (standards like sunlight, fire, silver, salt, jade, tungsten, etc). When you come into contact with the source of another type of Bane or Ban, you can apply it to your Buster once in the next 24 hours (if you have the source on you, of course, you can just apply it again immediately and reflexively).
