Gifted to Princesses of Mirrors—allows Crafts to be used instead of Science

You may summon multiple copies of an object or actor. All of these copies are phantasmal duplicates, and the original is not moved from its point of origin (but an actor still has to be willing to be summoned if you ask.) After accumulating enough successes to summon the thing, you may immediately apply remaining successes to the next copy, and the next, continuing to roll for as long as you want to keep making copies.
