Gifted to all Princesses in addition to their default Upgrade.

Your blessing may now include “Any attempt to use magic by and/or on the bearer has a dice penalty of -2, and an additional -1 for each charge spent on this Upgrade.” If magic used on the bearer is penalized, then any magic effect that would encompass the bearer is affected, even if they are not the primary target. For example, if someone tries to scry on the room a person is in, and the bearer is also in that room, then the roll is penalized.

Your trigger may now include the use of a magical spell or ability, even if you can’t otherwise perceive it.
