Gifted to Princesses of Tears—allows Crafts to be used instead of Occult

Your blessing may now include “Anyone or anything who sets off the trigger is repelled away from the bearer.” People are thrown backwards if they do not succeed on a Strength + Athletics -2 roll with an additional -1 for each charge spent on this Upgrade. If the bearer is not firmly secured to the ground, they will be thrown the same distance in the opposite direction.
