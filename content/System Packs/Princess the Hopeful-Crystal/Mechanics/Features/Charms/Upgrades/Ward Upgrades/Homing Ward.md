You may attach the talisman to a bearer as a ranged attack using Athletics or Firearms. This attack doesn’t ignore the target’s Defense and has a range of 10/20/40. If a casting of Ward requires you to place more than one talisman, you can send them all out at once, but each ranged attack is -1 for each talisman past the first.
