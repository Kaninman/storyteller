This is an advanced Upgrade. It counts as two Upgrades when applied to a Charm.

This is an enhanced version of Magic Ward, and must be applied to a cast that also has Magic Ward applied. The blessing of Magic Ward now reads “Reduce the successes of all magical effects used by or on the bearer (other than this one) by 2, plus an additional 1 for each charge spent on this Upgrade. Existing, ongoing magic effects are reduced in successes under this blessing and if the effect’s successes become 0 or less, it’s temporarily suppressed until the blessing runs out.”

That “by or on” is not a “by and/or” on; if you apply this it has to work in both directions.

If you choose to make Suppressing Ward oppose the All-Consuming Darkness Condition (p. 274), then it doesn’t oppose any other magic, and it can’t lower the natural background level of Darkness below 2, but it can negate stacks of the Condition that were artificially imposed by magic or go above 2.
Each stack you negate is considered to have taken 3 successes. Creatures with the Pure Darkness Dread Power (p. 150) get a Clash of Wills to decide if they can benefit from the power.
