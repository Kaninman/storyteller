This is an advanced Upgrade. It counts as two Upgrades when applied to a Charm.

Your blessing may now include “Cast a Charm that you pre-loaded when you cast Ward.” Spend all the resources, choose who and what will be targeted by the Charm, and determine the dice pool when loading that Charm, but don’t roll the dice pool until activated.

Unless you pay the resources for the Charm multiple times when loading it, this blessing won’t go off again when the trigger happens again.
