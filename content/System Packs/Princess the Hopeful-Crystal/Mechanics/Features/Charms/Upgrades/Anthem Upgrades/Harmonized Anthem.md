_“If you’re ever in trouble, I’ll be there on the double, just send for me oh baby!”_
Your Anthem applies two different Stirring Performance Tilts simultaneously. They must be distinct feelings, but any bonuses and penalties from them stack so long as an action fulfills / runs counter to both of them.

When you do this, the same Upgrades apply to each Stirring Performance, but you divvy up the charges separately.
