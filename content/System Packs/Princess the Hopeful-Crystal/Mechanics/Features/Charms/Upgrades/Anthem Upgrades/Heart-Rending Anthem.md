Gifted to Princesses of Tears—allows Wits to be used instead of Presence
_“Feels like pins and needles in my heart, so long I can feel it tearing me apart…”_

Anyone in the audience acting against the feeling you inspire requires a Resolve + Composure roll contested against this Charm. On a failure, the action costs a Willpower. This can only drain one Willpower per scene, plus an additional Willpower for each charge spent.
