“Girl, I can thrill you more than any ghoul can ever dare try!”

You can lead your audience in specific actions—anyone under the effect of your Stirring Performance who doesn’t resist can use their turn to mimic your actions exactly. The audience will use your Skills and Attributes to make rolls this way, but they can’t do supernatural things they don’t otherwise have the ability for.

If you apply this Upgrade, it costs two charges for each turn to extend your Stirring Performance
