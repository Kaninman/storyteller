“Put another dime in the jukebox, baby.”

Your performance can be captured in some form that the audience can access later. The performance will only apply the effect of Anthem once; after that, the performance may be stirring but it won’t apply a Tilt.

This means you can make a musical or oratory performance through a record of your sound and voice. It also means you can make your performance in the form of any other art that can convey your emotions and doesn’t require you to be there to perform it: a sculpture, a written poem, even a loaf of bread baked with absolute care and passion.
