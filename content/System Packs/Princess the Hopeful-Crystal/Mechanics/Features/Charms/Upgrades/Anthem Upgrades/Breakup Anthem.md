_“I don’t wanna be a fool for you / in this game for two / so I’m leaving you behind!”_
Your Anthem can reflexively trigger a Clash of Wills against someone using a supernatural ability on or in your audience, even if it doesn’t directly oppose the Anthem. If you win the Clash, the opposing spell or ability fizzles out, unable to throw off the groove. This only works once per scene, plus one additional time for each charge spent, and you must spend a Wisp each time it triggers.
