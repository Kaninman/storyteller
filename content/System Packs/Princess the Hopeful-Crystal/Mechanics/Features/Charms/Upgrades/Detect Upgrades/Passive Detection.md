Use this when you ask one of your questions and get no useful answer. This is usually because you asked for the nearest example of something but there are none in your effective range. The effect remains active, and when some- thing that does match your question pops into your effective range, you become immediately aware of it. Using this Upgrade, you can intentionally set a certain threshold of size or severity for the Detection to go off; you don’t have to consume your Passive Detect Danger to find out there’s a rusty nail in someone’s yard.
