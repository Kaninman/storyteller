Gifted to Princesses of Storms—allows Brawl or Weaponry to be used instead of Investigation
You may choose the query option:
★ “...what is the best way to disable, destroy, or negate the defenses of…”
Remember that Detect can only use the senses in your sense option. You can’t use this to determine the Bane or Ban of any given entity with no other information—but if there’s a slight hint or something nearby that can lead to a clue, Detect will be able to pick it up even if it’s too fast or small for normal perception.
