This is an advanced Upgrade. It counts as two Upgrades when applied to a Charm.
You gain precognition, the ability to look into the future—or the most likely possible future, anyway. You gain the sense option:
★ “By looking into the future…”
By default, you may look into the future a number of turns up to your Inner Light. You may spend 1 Willpower and five minutes casting this Charm to look up to that many hours into the future, or you may spend 2 Willpower, 2 Wisps, and an hour casting this Charm to look up to that many days in the future. Reduce the range for vagueness like normal.

This Charm only reveals the most likely future based on current trends and probability; it can change based on people’s actions and decisions. It never takes the information that it gives you into account: each potential future shown by Detect Future assumes the caster fails to get a single success on their Detect Future casting, and that any subsequent supernatural ability to detect the future also fails.
