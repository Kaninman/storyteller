Allows Occult to be used instead of Investigation
You may choose the subject option:
★ “...an ephemeral being or area?”
This allows you to see into Twilight on a “frequency” you choose, and can extend your range into another realm of your choosing that physically maps to reality (like the spirit world, Hedge, Underworld, and Dark World). The latter function applies a penalty to the casting roll equal to the strength of the barrier separating worlds, like the Gaunt- let. The strength of the barrier separating the Dark World is 7, minus two for each stack of All-Consuming Darkness, so it’s a 3 most of the time and 1 in a Bleed.
