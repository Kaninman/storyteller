Visible light consists of electromagnetic radiation at a wavelength of about 400 to 700 nanometers. Outside that are infrared and ultraviolet light, and beyond those are gamma radiation, radio, and television signals. You can interpret all of those as easily as you see light. You may now pick the sense option:
★ “Looking over the entire electromagnetic spectrum…”
You can listen to unencrypted information, like radio waves, as if they were just normal sound. If you pick up encrypted data, like from a cellular or wireless network transmission, you can see or record it in encrypted form but it won’t help you crack the code.
