You rapidly swap through your arsenal of weapons as you execute a stylish attack combo, leaving your opponents dizzied and helpless against your onslaught. Add one additional weapon to your total attack, plus one more for each charge spent, up to the total number of weapons you have in reach or available (on your person, laying around the environment, or currently able to Conjure into your hands). Your strike uses the highest Damage rating of all of the weapons you used, gets +1 die for each weapon used, and applies all special properties of every single weapon you use in the combo to the entire attack. At the end of the combo, you may choose to have any of the weapons you used be the one active and in your hand
