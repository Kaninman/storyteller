Gifted to Princesses of Mirrors

When you want to fight, you get a fight. You can force the target into a position where they must fight you; when you hit, the target gains a stack of the Challenged Tilt (p. 268), which penalizes their next die roll by -2 unless that action is directly attacking you with a weapon or their fists. For each charge spent, the penalty increases by another -1.
