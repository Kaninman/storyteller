Your attacks bring your opponents into your grasp, the perfect place to perform a spinning piledriver. Your strike can take the form of a grapple; on a hit, you immediately may attempt one of the grapple maneuvers (CofD p. 89).

Your opponent’s roll to contest the grapple is at -2, plus an additional -1 for each charge you spend. Upgrades like Gunslinger Style or Wide Sweep can allow you to initiate a grapple at a range where you can’t physically hold someone. The Upgrade still works to do one maneuver, but at the start of the opponent’s turn, if you don’t have a way of maintaining the hold, they automatically and reflexively break free.
