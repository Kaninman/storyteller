The entity has +1 on _all_ rolls to interact with Travelers in the Dreamlands for each stack of Galemark the Traveler has; this part of the ability costs no Essence.

By spending 1 Essence, the entity infuses an interaction with the narrative power of the Gales to sweep people up. As long as the entity is engaged with its targets as part of a narrative (which is any kind of continuous action), multiple people in the interaction are subject to the Gales. Targets will become aware that someone is using the Gales on them if this roll is frequently invoked, but that won’t stop it from working, though escaping or beating up the entity usually will.
