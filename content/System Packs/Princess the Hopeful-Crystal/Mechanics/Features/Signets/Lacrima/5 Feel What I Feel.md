When you take this Signet, you design a Nightscape driven by your sorrow instead of your fear. Once per chapter, roll a Belief Compromise at the Pragmatist Tier to activate this Signet (If your Belief is 1, you don’t have to roll). For the rest of the scene, your surroundings become that Nightscape, with Potency equal to your Inner Light. It doesn’t come with Nightmares, but it does have dice pools that will enforce the psychodrama, and can cast your Charms as if they were Dread Powers. You cannot directly command the Nightscape and it only acts to enforce the psychodrama.
