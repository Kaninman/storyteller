You may spend 1 Wisp to be cloaked in ravens for one turn. Birds of shadow and regret swarm around you out to a distance equal to 4 times your Inner Light in meters. They apply the Swarm (p. 273) and Poor Light (p. 273) Tilts to everyone within this area but you, and you may shield allies from damage with your Defiance. You can reflexively disperse into your swarm and reform anywhere in it as part of your movement, and if you keep it active on subsequent turns, you can center it around anyone who started the turn in the swarm.
