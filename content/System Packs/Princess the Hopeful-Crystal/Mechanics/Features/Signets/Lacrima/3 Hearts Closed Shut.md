You know how to channel sorrow into action. Whenever a target gains a Condition as part of failing a Breaking Point, you may spend a Willpower point to guide their aching heart. For as long as they have the Condition, social maneuvers against them have Average impression, except yours, which have Perfect impression instead, and any further Breaking Points the target would suffer are delayed until the Condition is resolved. The Raven doesn’t have to cause the initial Breaking Point herself, but it’s often the simplest option.
