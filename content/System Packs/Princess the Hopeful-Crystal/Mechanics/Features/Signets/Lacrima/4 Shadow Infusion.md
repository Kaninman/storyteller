You gain the “Shadow” Infusion Upgrade for two of your Charms:
“This Charm is infused with the power of Lacrima, granting it an ability related to darkness, shadow, silence, sorrow, or Darkspawn.”

_Examples: An Anthem that silences all other communication in the area. A Step that allows you to walk into one shadow and emerge from another. A Conjure that is attuned to all types of Darkspawn you’ve met or a Plea that can motivate Darkspawn. A Buster or Kiai attack that leaves targets blinded by clinging darkness. A dark Restoration that ‘heals’ away false hope and leaves sorrow._
