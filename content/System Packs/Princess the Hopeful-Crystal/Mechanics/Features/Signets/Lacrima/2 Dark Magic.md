Choose two Charms to become Shadow-Attuned. Shadow-Attuned Charms act through or on darkness, silence, sorrow, and non-hostile Darkspawn.

_Examples: An Illusion that creates only silhouettes (so they can’t look like a real object observed directly), an Anthem that instills sadness (but can’t provoke other emotions that might be useful), or a Step that shrouds your actions in inky darkness (but does not function while in direct sunlight)._
