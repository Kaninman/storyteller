You can’t die. You’re not done yet. There is more violence you must visit upon the Darkness. Your soul claws its way back to the world through your weak, dissolving flesh, and you return in a corrosive burst of undying hate.

When you die, this Signet triggers, and you return to life. You can’t choose to hold off on it so someone with Desperate Restoration can revive you. All of your damage is cleared, even if it’s Resistant. Recover all of your Willpower and Wisps. Clear all physical and supernatural Conditions. You assume your Transformed state if you weren’t in it already. You automatically Flare as many times as you can (and since you do it after you fill your Wisps the new capacity you get isn’t filled by default). Your original body gets back up and repairs itself with a sickening burst of caustic energy, or your old body dissolves into an acrid slurry and you emerge (with all of your possessions and Regalia) in an explosion of pure murderous intent.

You also gain a Persistent Mental Condition due to your harrowing kiss with Death and burst of self-hatred at your own weakness. You lose one Willpower dot, one Belief, one maximum Belief, and this Signet. You can regain the Signet, Willpower dot, and Belief like normal, but the highest Belief you can ever reach is permanently reduced. If you intentionally activate this Signet by killing yourself, then you don’t lose the Signet or the maximum Belief and the Mental Condition you gain isn’t Persistent, though it
will be long-lasting.
