You gain the “Corrosive” Infusion Upgrade for two of your Charms:
“This Charm is infused with the power of Tempesta, granting it an ability related to acid, corrosion, annihilation, violence, or hatred.”

_Examples: Buster and Kiai attacks that leave absolutely nothing behind of those they kill. Restoration and Pleas that eat away at people inside. Anthems that stir listeners to immediate violence. A Force locus that corrodes whatever it grasps._
