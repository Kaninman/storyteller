You may embrace an attempt to harm you in order to empower your revenge against it. When someone makes a successful attack or hostile action against you, spend a Wisp. If it was an attack, then you can’t apply Defiance to it; if it was another hostile action, it becomes an automatic Exceptional Success.

The next time you take a similar action against the same person, you automatically gain successes equal to the original successes of the roll made against you (ie, you don’t automatically get 5 on an attack roll), and if it was a non-attack action you automatically get an Exceptional Success.

Your grudge may last up to one day per Inner Light you have.
