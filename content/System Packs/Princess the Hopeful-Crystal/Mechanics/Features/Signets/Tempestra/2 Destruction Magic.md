Choose two Charms to become Acid-Attuned. Acid-Attuned Charms act through or on acid, corrosion, destruction, or hatred.

_Examples: A Conjure that rips objects through caustic holes you erode in your surroundings (but damages the things you summon), a Ward that annihilates what triggers it (even if it’s something you don’t need to destroy), or an Anthem that stokes hatred (but can’t be used to calm people down when you really need that option)._
