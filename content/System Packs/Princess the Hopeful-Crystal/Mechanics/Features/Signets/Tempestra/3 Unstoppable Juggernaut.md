You are always immune to wound penalties and automatically succeed at any attempt to remain conscious when your rightmost Health box is filled with Bashing damage.

You may spend a Willpower to enhance this ability for one scene. If you do, all damage penalties are reversed to bonuses, and when you take overflow damage that upgrades from Bashing to Lethal or Lethal to Aggravated, you only take one point of damage maximum (after applying Defiance).
