You can travel from one location to another with great speed while Transformed. Spend a Willpower and about two minutes to activate this Signet, during which you elevate to a height of 500 yards (about as high as a hot-air balloon) to begin your journey. While airborne, you move at about 120 MPH (about the speed of a Cessna).

While airborne, you can’t cast spells, make attacks, or do anything requiring concentration. You can communicate, take pictures and otherwise observe and enjoy the trip. If you take any damage, the Signet ends, but you are protected from cold and atmosphere while you’re up there. When the Signet ends, willingly or unwillingly, you float down to the ground over the course of one turn.
