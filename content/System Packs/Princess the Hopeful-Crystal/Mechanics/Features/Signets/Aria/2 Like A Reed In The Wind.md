You can adapt your magic to new situations that arise. On your turn, you may reflexively change the Upgrades on one of your ongoing or extended-action Charms without spending Wisps or time to cast it again. The effect proceeds from there as if you’d always cast it with the new Upgrade(s), but previous effects aren’t undone (you’re not changing the past, you’re hot-swapping in the moment).

You can do this a number of times per chapter equal to your Aria dots. You can’t swap away Upgrades that only altered your initial casting (like Ready Conjure) or Upgrades that give the Charm an ongoing effect (like Round Trip and BOTTOMLESS INFINITY BUSTER!).
