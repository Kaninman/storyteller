Even while not transformed, when someone else in sensory range chooses a person for something, and you were a valid choice, you may spend a Wisp—if you do, they actually chose you instead, whether by their own caprice or by a random selection arriving on you. This only works if you would have been a credible choice anyway; you can get a monster to attack you instead of someone else, you can be picked as a volunteer, but you can’t make someone overlook far more qualified candidates for a position. You can’t win the lottery with this (each ball is a separate result and none are associated with you), but you can win a raffle with one ticket.
