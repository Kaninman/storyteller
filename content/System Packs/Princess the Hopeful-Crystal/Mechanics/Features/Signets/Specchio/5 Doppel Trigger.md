You’re the only person who can save the world—it simply needs more of you. As an instant action, you may spend a Wisp to create a copy of yourself out of crackling energy for one turn in combat, or one roll out- side of combat; this is not an illusion, this is a doppelganger made of pure power. If you spend Wisps on subsequent turns/rolls, the Doppelganger is maintained; you don’t resummon it and it doesn’t take an action. The first time you cast this Charm in a scene, gain a Fractured condition of your choice. The Doppelganger has all of your abilities except for ones that would be able to create more Doppelgangers, and uses your resources for its abilities, but has its own Health.

You do not fully control the Doppelganger—though it has your personality, it is under a permanent Dissociated Condition (p. 268), so isn’t always thinking clearly and precisely about how to best aid you, and it will force the events around it to fit a comforting mental narrative. It won’t believe itself to be the “real you” and refuse to assist you, though, so you don’t have to worry about that.
