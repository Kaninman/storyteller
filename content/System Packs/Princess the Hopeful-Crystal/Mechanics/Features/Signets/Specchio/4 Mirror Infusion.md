You gain the “Mirror” Infusion Upgrade for two of your Charms:
“This Charm is infused with the power of Specchio, granting it an ability related to reflection, duplication, or light manipulation.”

_Examples: A Ward, Force, Restoration, or Kiai that copies an opponent’s action and repeats it back at them. An Anthem, Plea, Buster, or Illusion that has twice as many (non-overlapping) targets or affected areas. A Step that lets you split into two and then later declare which one of you was the “real” one (like Tricky Illusion). A Detect that gives you complete visual awareness of everything in your radius as light is bent toward you._
