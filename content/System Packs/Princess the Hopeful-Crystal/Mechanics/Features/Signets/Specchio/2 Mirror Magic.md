Choose two Charms to become Mirror-Attuned. Mirror-Attuned Charms act through or on reflective surfaces, light, or your own vanity.

_Examples: An Illusion that refracts and redirects light around you (but can’t make images of things that you don’t have near you), a Plea that exalts your own deeds and prowess (but can’t motivate people to do things that don’t directly appear to involve you), or a Detect that works through asking a magic mirror (but the voice of the guy inside doesn’t always know exactly what you mean)._
