When you cast a Charm, you may choose to infuse it with so much magic that it declares everything about itself to the world. You shout it out with such force that anyone who can see or hear any part of its effects knows exactly who cast it (you), what Charm it is, what upgrades you applied, what your dice pool was for it, and what each of those dice came from. If you do, add your dots in Fuoco to the roll, and double the dice you get from your Inner Light. You can only use this once per scene.
