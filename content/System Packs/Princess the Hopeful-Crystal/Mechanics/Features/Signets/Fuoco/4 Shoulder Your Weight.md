When you attempt to remove any ongoing negative condition from another, mundane or magical, and fail, you may spend a Wisp. If you do, then the condition is removed from them, but inflicted on you instead. At the end of the scene, the condition reverts back to its original owner if possible. If you successfully remove a positive condition, you can spend a Wisp and take it for yourself for the rest of the scene.
