Once per scene, spend a Wisp and a Willpower to straight up set the world on fire. Everyone around you has one turn to run like hell, as over the course of one turn the area within 3 meters per Inner Light you have is engulfed in a blaze. This is an Inferno-sized fire (3 damage) with the intensity of a torch (+1 damage from heat). You and anyone or anything you designate are immune to the fire’s damage. If you have any Charms that work through fire, you can cast them through your inferno and reduce the cost by 1 Wisp. The fire will extinguish once it runs out of fuel, won’t expand beyond twice its initial size, and will probably cause a hell of a lot of damage to whatever environment you use it in.
