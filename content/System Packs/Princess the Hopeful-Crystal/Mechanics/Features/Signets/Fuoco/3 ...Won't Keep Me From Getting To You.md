No mountain, no valley, no winter’s cold, no wind, no rain. When you have this Signet you are always completely immune to non-magical fire damage. You can spend a Wisp to negate any other environmental Tilt you’re subject to for the rest of the scene, or to add your Fuoco as armor against a magical fire attack.
