An adventurer doesn’t have time to make sure the situation is optimized before rushing in! You may spend a Wisp when making a roll to negate dice penalties from Tilts, non-Persistent Conditions, unusual circumstances, and being unskilled. The number of dice you cancel out is equal to your Fuoco dots +1. This can’t end up providing a bonus.
