Once per scene, at the start of a turn, spend 3 Wisps and one Willpower to jostle time. Everyone but you and the people you designate, acting in initiative order, has to declare what they are doing that turn. They cannot deviate from that plan when their turn comes up, even if you have done something that would otherwise change their priorities.
