Choose two Charms to become Water-Attuned. Water-Attuned Charms act through or on water, ice, currents, or coldness.

**Examples:** _A Detect that searches for answers by scrying into a pool of water (but needs a calm pool of water to see into), a Ward applied by sprinkling holy water (but you have to make sure it doesn’t dry out), or an Illusion that manifests images from cold mist (but doesn’t work if the area is too warm for the mist to form)._
