Reflexively spend a Wisp to assume a liquid form for one turn. You retain the same shape and features, but you and all your gear are a translucent liquid, the color of your choice, and you control your shape as if it was your natural body, flowing into or through anywhere that isn’t watertight. Any physical attack relying on kinetic force deals only one point of bashing damage to you.
