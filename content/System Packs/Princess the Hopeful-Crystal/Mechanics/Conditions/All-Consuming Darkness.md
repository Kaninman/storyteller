The taint of the Darkness threatens this place. The Light of Hope pushes it back, but the Darkness always pressures that little bubble of illumination inward as hard as it can. For each stack of All-Consuming Darkness in the area, Horrors with Pure Darkness increase their Potency by 1, and the use of Pure Darkness by those who don’t have the Dread Power itself get +1 die.

**Causes / Removal:** The strength of the Darkness in an area, both in terms of its active servants and its grip on the people who live there, determines the stacks of All-Consuming Darkness. Defeating Darkspawn, Nightscapes, and Defilers in the area can reduce stacks of this Condition; so can uplifting and inspiring people to resist fear and let in hope. Increasing the grip of fear on an area, opening Bleeds, allowing major physical manifestations of Darkness to exist unchallenged, or the direct use of Dread Powers like Fortress of Terror (p. 189) can all add stacks.
