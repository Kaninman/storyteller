The Gales of the Dreamlands howl in your ears, and you are succumbing to its dream-logic. If you have one stack, you’re fine. For every stack after the first, you have a -1 penalty to recall specific information from outside the Dreamlands while inside the Dreamlands, and vice-versa. Also a -1 penalty to every single action you take if it’s not in line with fully embodying your drive.

**Cause:** You were subject to [[The Gales]], rolled Belief, and gained more successes than your current stacks of Galemark.

**Beat:** Suffer a significant setback or risk because of your compromised memory.

**Ending:** Lose 1 stack of Galemark when you wake up after the night where you gained one or more stacks of Galemark. Lose 1 stack of Galemark for each night thereafter you choose to go without dreaming, and forfeit the Willpower you would normally gain.
