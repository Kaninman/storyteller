What if you’re not good enough? You failed. Maybe failing is all you can do. Your character, after a Compromise or a close brush with one, doubts her own abilities and worth so much it weakens her magic.

**Causes:** You’ve recently made a roll for a Belief Compromise.

**Resolution:** Lock out access to a single relevant Charm for one scene; you’re so inside your own head that your magic sputters and fizzles, and you have to use other means to get what you want.
