Anyone who attempts to change the Prisoner’s mind is actually trying to destroy it. Once this Condition is active, the Nightscape can directly and actively interfere with intruders by deploying its Dread Powers and using its active dice pools at any time, not just when appropriate to the psychodrama. The cost of each Dread Power now increases by 1 each time it’s used in a given scene. In combat, the Nightscape has a number of actions per turn equal to one third of its Potency, rounded up, only one of which can be used to activate a Dread Power. Its Initiative is always +0.
