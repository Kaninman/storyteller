Anyone who attempts to change the Prisoner’s mind is trying to trick them into harming themselves. They have to be stopped. Once this Condition is active, Nightmares actively hunt down intruders in the Nightscape instead of simply embodying their roles in the psychodrama. When a Nightmare attacks, the psychodrama doesn’t “notice” how it disrupts the narrative, nor will it “notice” direct attempts to stop it—you’ll have to play into the psychodrama and fight at the same time.
