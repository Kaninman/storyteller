You have flared your Belief to strengthen your Inner Light, investing part of your self-image in your ability to triumph. Increase your Inner Light by +1. Note a goal when you gain this Condition; if you already have any stacks of this Condition, the goal must be more specific and more difficult than the existing ones.

**Cause:** You flared your Belief in pursuit of a greater goal.

**Resolution:** Succeed in the goal you set when you got this Condition. Gain a Luminous Beat instead of a normal one.

**Failure:** Fail to achieve the goal you set when you got this Condition. Lose this Condition, do not gain a Beat, and lose a Belief dot.
