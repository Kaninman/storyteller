This Nightmare has returned to a disrupted physical form. It is [[Coalesce|Coalesced]], it cannot activate other [[Condition Base.base#Manifestations|Manifestations]], and it loses all bonuses from the [[Pure Darkness]] Dread Power.

**Cause:** Forcing a Nightmare out of a more advanced condition based on Coalesce, or ending the Menace Condition a Nightmare used to Coalesce, will inflict Undermined Nightmare.
