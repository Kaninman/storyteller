This Nightmare belongs to a Nightscape that was destroyed without resolving it. It is permanently Coalesced and suffers Essence Bleed. It cannot return to its native Nightscape to reform.

**Condition:** Rogue Nightmare is caused when a Nightscape is destroyed or its prisoner killed.

**Ending:** This Condition only ends with the destruction of
the Nightmare.
