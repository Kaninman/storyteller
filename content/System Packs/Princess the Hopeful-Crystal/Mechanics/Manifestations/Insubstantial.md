This Nightmare is in a Twilight state, becoming intangible while retaining the ability to use Numina and Dread Powers. All rules for creatures in Twilight now apply.

**Cause:** The Nightmare had the Insubstantial Manifestation, which listed a prerequisite, and activated this Manifestation while Coalesced and while the prerequisite was present or true.

**Ending:** Undoing the prerequisite ends this Condition, reverts the Nightmare to Coalesced, and gives it the Undermined Nightmare Tilt.
