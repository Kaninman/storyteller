This area is ripe with potential for a Nightmare to harvest Essence by enacting its Dread. An appropriate Nightmare may Coalesce here.

**Cause:** A group of people vulnerable to the fear the Nightmare embodies, or one person currently very worried about that fear, none of whom believe they can protect themselves from it.

**Ending:** Evacuating endangered civilians, moving the Nightmare to a location without them, or calming them down and convincing them there is a way to protect themselves from the source of fear will remove the Menace Condition. If the Nightmare is Coalesced, removing Menace inflicts the Undermined Nightmare Tilt.
