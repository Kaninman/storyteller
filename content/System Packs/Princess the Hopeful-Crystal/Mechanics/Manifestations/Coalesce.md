This Nightmare assumes a physical form, matter congealing out of [[Darkness|the Darkness]] that underlies our reality. It can move, act, be attacked, and employ all of its [[Dread Powers]] and [[Numina]]; once embodied, it may also attempt to [[Possessed|Possess]], [[Tether]], or go [[Insubstantial]].

**Cause:** The Nightmare spent Essence equal to twice its Potency in an area with the appropriate [[Menace]] condition.

**Ending:** The Coalesce Condition only ends when the scene ends, or when the Nightmare suffers enough damage to discorporate it. If the Menace condition that it relied on is removed, Coalesce does not end, but the Nightmare suffers the [[Undermined Nightmare|Undermined Nightmare Tilt]].
