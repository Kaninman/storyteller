# Mechanics
The dhampir binds another’s fate to her own. She must touch the wound she inflicts upon herself to someone else’s fresh injury, which must have caused lethal or aggravated damage, sealing the deal in blood. If the victim is unwilling, she may need to roll to touch him first using Dexterity + Brawl – Defense. Then she must speak an oath aloud, making her intentions to
forge the connection clear.

>**Cost:** 2 lethal damage
>**Dice Pool:** Presence + Resolve vs. Composure + Supernatural Tolerance
>**Time:** Instant action

# Roll Results
**Dramatic Failure:** The dhampir binds herself to her victim, but the effects aren’t mutual; only the dhampir gains the Blood Siblings Condition (below).

**Failure:** Fate ignores the dhampir’s oath.

**Success:** The dhampir and her victim both gain the Blood Siblings Condition.

**Exceptional Success:** As success, and the victim’s Blood Siblings Condition doesn’t lapse on its own. It must be resolved normally.
