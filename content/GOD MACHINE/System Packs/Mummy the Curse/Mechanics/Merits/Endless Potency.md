The immortal channels her Sekhem into inborn ability. When purchasing this Merit, choose an Attribute. By spending a dot of Sekhem, the character increases that Attribute by the Merit’s dot value, including derived traits, for the remainder of the scene. Activating this Merit can take her above her normal Attribute dot limit (usually 5). Invested immortals may spend Pillars to activate this Merit instead of Sekhem. This may be used once per Attribute per scene.
Notes: You may take this Merit multiple times, each purchase representing a different Attribute.
