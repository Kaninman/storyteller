The dhampir is always aware when anyone present in the scene suffers a breaking point that aligns with the Theme. She may spend a Willpower point to intuit the results of the roll and choose another character present in the scene upon whom to inflict the Guilty Condition (Vampire: The Requiem Second Edition, p. 304), verbally cursing him for playing a role in the fateful event. She may do so even when she herself suffers an appropriate breaking point.
