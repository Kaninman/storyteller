You’ve had the breath knocked out of you, and now you’re gasping for air. You have -3 dice to physical actions, your base Speed is halved, and you must succeed on a reflexive Stamina roll (without the penalty) to be able to talk.

**Cause:** You got hit right in the solar plexus, like that little arch where your ribs meet up above your abdomen. A character can cause Winded by aiming for the chest and dealing at least as much damage as his Size; weapons that deal bashing damage add double their damage modifier for this purpose.

**Ending:** Winded ends when you take an instant action to
catch your breath.
