This is an advanced Upgrade. It counts as two Upgrades when applied to a Charm.

During Step, you may spend one Wisp to enter a Twilight state for one turn. In Twilight, you are completely intangible to the physical world, and can interact with ephemeral beings in Twilight as if they were entirely physical.
