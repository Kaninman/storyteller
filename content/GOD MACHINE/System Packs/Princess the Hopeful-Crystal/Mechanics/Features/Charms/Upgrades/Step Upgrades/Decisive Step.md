Gifted to Princesses of Jewels

You move yourself, and nobody else does. During Step, you selectively have the mass and inertia of a boulder that weighs one ton per Inner Light you have as you need it. It takes no conscious effort to determine what purposes you have your normal weight for; you can render yourself immune to someone’s attempts to throw you off of a wooden scaffold because they can’t lift a ton but you won’t fall through the wooden scaffold because you only weigh 125 pounds. In addition to the other possibilities this opens up, nothing can grapple you or move you from your position unless it could move a multi-ton weight, and you do not take damage from impact, either from you hitting the ground, or incidental collisions with your environment.
