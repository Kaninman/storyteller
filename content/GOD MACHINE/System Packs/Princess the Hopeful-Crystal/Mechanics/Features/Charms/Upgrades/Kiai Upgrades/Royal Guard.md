Gifted to Princesses of Jewels
The target of your strike may be a source of incoming damage; this attack becomes reflexive when you’re defending against an attack. Your blow parries the damage, reducing the successes of the incoming attack roll by your Charm’s successes. You do not add your weapon’s damage to this, and if you fail to negate all the successes on the incoming attack, you will still take the guaranteed damage from the weapon damage rating.

This works on absolutely any form of incoming damage that your strike can interact with and that you can see coming. Deflect gunfire and slice the bullets from the air. Shoot a rocket out of the sky. Cut a falling skyscraper in half and shout “RULES OF NATURE!”

Activating this Upgrade costs 1 additional Wisp unless you are taking a Dodge action. If you have any other effects that allow you to counterattack after a successful dodge, whether from [[Counter Stance]], a Fighting Style, or something else, that attack has all of the same Kiai Upgrades as this one.
