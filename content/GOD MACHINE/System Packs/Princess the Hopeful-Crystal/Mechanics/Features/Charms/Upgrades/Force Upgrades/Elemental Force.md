When you take this Upgrade, choose a type of material or energy. It is often the same element linked to your Queen and her Signet, but doesn’t have to be. When you apply this Upgrade, the locus only interacts with that material or energy, is made of that material or energy, has all the properties of that material or energy, and manipulates that material or energy as if it was weightless, frictionless, and requires no roll.

You may purchase this Upgrade multiple times to choose multiple elements. When you do, it only costs 1 XP each additional time.
