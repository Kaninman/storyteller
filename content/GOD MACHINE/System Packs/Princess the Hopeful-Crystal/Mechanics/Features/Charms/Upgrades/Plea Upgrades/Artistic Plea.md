Allows—and requires—Expression to be used instead of Persuasion

The purpose of art is to stir the emotions, make you think, make you feel. Art can speak like words can’t. You may beseech in the form of a piece of art, or a recorded or written message. The audience must still spend as much time observing or engaging with it as they would otherwise listen to you, and you have a stacking -1 penalty to each roll to represent the difficulty of communicating without direct feedback from the audience. Each person may only be affected by a given Artistic Plea once. The work of art loses its magic after a number of days equal to your Inner Light.
