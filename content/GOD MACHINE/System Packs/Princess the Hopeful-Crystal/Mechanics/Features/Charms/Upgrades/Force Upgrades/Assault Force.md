Gifted to Princesses of Storms—allows Weaponry or Brawl to be used instead of Crafts
Your locus can manipulate to perform an attack. If this requires you to aim the attack from somewhere other than the locus’s location, this attack is at -2 and the target always applies their full Defense even if something would otherwise reduce or ignore it. If you’re not using a weapon, the Force is a weapon with a rating of 0L or 0B.
