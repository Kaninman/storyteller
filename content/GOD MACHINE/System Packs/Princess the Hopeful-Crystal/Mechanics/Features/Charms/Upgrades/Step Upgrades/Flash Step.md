This is an advanced Upgrade. It counts as two Upgrades when applied to a Charm.

During Step, you can spend extra Wisps to reflexively move a distance equal to your Movement rating. As long as there is no barrier in the way, you disappear and reappear with a “zwee” without physically crossing the distance. Invoking this ability costs 1 Wisp, plus one additional Wisp for each time you’ve used it this turn.
