Gifted to Princesses of Storms—allows Dexterity to be used instead of Manipulation

You may now summon objects that are actively being attended by someone else, and unwilling actors. The casting becomes contested by the Dexterity + Resolve of someone who is attending the object, or the Dexterity + Resolve + Potency of an unwilling actor. If the other side accumulates successes equal to your Inner Light, the spell fails. Conjuring an unwilling actor doesn’t cause them to be delayed for a turn, they are able to act immediately.
