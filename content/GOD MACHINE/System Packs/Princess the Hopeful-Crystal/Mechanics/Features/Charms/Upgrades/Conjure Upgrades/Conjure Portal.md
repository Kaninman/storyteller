This is an advanced Upgrade. It counts as two Upgrades
when applied to a Charm.

Your valid objects now include “a location” that you are otherwise attuned to. You create a portal linking that location and the area where you summon it that lasts until the end of the scene; anything that goes in one portal comes out the other as if there was no physical distance between them.

The portal doesn’t have a fixed size. You can choose any Size you want for the portal, and that will be the maximum Size of thing that can fit through it. Using Conjure Portal requires you to spend a Wisp for each roll you make on the extended action, not just the initial cast; if you went over your free Upgrade capacity and had to spend extra Wisps on the initial cast you don’t need to spend them again.
