You can attempt to interrupt a target’s action by hitting them first. When you cast Kiai with Opportunity Strike, don’t roll it yet. At any point afterwards, until you take another action, you may attempt to interrupt a target’s action by hitting them first with your strike. Your attack comes after the target declares their action, but before they resolve it. If you hit them, they end their movement, then they continue the same action if possible. Unlike delaying, this doesn’t lower your place in the Initiative order in subsequent rounds.
