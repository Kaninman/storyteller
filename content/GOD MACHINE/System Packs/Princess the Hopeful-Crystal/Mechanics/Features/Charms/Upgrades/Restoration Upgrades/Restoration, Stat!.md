Get moving! You need this patient stable and healthy yesterday! Restoration, Stat! allows you to mend an injury in the blink of an eye. You no longer forfeit your Defense to mend as the effect happens instantly rather than over the course of a turn; you may cast this Charm at any point in initiative order; and the process of your mending is so fast it can send things flying, which you can use to your advantage with several Upgrades. Used with an Upgrade that increases the interval between rolls to 24 hours, you can instead change the interval to 8 hours for injuries not incurred in the current scene, or 10 minutes for those incurred in this current scene.
