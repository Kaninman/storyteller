If everything you flickered was clothing, Regalia, or some kind of outfit, and the image was also clothing or some kind of outfit, you can maintain it reflexively. It doesn’t count against your maximum number of images, and it can’t be magically dispelled. You can cover multiple different people’s outfits if you all scooch together.
