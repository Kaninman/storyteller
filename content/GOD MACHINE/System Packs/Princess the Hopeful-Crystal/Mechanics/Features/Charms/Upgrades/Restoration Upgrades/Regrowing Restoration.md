Gifted to Princesses of Flowers—allows Resolve to be used instead of Intelligence

Nature and life are able to bounce back and flourish of their own accord, as long as you help start the process. Once you cast Restoration to mend a patient or object, you do not need to remain next to them and take your action to continue casting; you can continue the extended roll while applying your Defense, moving away, or doing anything else. If you cast another Charm, or if either you or the patient is disrupted by taking damage, the Charm ends.
