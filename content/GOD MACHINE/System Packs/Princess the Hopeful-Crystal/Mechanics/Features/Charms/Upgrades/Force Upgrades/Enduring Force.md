Gifted to Princesses of Flowers—allows Stamina to be used instead of Strength
Your locus remains active while you aren’t concentrating on it, though you can’t give it new orders—it will stay stationary or repeat the last order given to it, and can’t adjust position or facing. Any dice pool for an action lowers by -1 on each subsequent turn it acts without your concentration, as things slip out of alignment.

When combined with Barrier, your Barrier will keep its Durability even without concentration but you still need to concentrate to refill its Structure.
