Gifted to Princesses of Storms—allows Intimidation to be used instead of Persuasion

When you apply this Upgrade, you don’t determine Impression based on how much the audience likes or respects you. Determine Impression based on how afraid the audience is of your ability to hurt them instead. You can apply Hard Leverage as part of a Plea. 

The “plea” part doesn’t come from the caster—it comes from the audience pleading for mercy.
