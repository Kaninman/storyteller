Gifted to Princesses of Mirrors

You can make a God-damn entrance, is what you can do. The first time someone sees you move with Step active, they make a Wits + Composure roll. If they fail to get higher successes than your Inner Light, you have taken them by surprise for this round even if they knew you were coming. This completely negates their Defense (even if they have an ability that lets them retain it against certain attacks), they cannot take actions against you, and they are unable to react to your actions by delaying their turn, in addition to any other implications of being surprised.

This Upgrade can only affect anyone once in a given scene.
