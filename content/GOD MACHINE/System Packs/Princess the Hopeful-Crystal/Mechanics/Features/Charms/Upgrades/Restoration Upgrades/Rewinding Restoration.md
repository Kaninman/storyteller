Gifted to Princesses of Spades—allows Dexterity to be used instead of Intelligence

If you want things to be like they were before, why not just… do that? Rewinding Restoration is able to mend injuries by rewinding time. A Rewinding Restoration still heals the same amount of damage, but does so by reverting the patient to a previous condition and location—meaning that all sorts of secondary effects, appearances, and even physical position get reset as well. Memory and mental resources are unaffected without another Upgrade that lets you do that. Each round you want to rewind a patient costs 1 success for the first, 2 for the second, etc. If the patient is unwilling, the first round costs successes equal to their Resolve and it goes up from there. It’s your responsibility to make note of where people were if you think you might rewind them.
