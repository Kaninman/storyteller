Why can’t we all just get along? A Calming Plea begs a stop to the violence. The request you ask for may now add “and please become affected by the Pacify Tilt, as will I.”

The Pacify Tilt (p. 270) causes a penalty to any hostile action equal to this Charm’s activation successes and trans-forms Failure into Dramatic Failure for affected rolls. Once someone suffers a Dramatic Failure, the Tilt expires from everyone. If you use Sweeping Plea, it also applies this Tilt to all of your allies.
