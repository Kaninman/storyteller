This is an advanced Upgrade. It counts as two Upgrades when applied to a Charm.

You can mimic and repeat an Instant action performed by an audience member under your Stirring Performance Tilt, so long as it’s something you understand how to do; you do not have to have a power or Merit you’d otherwise need.

Use the casting roll for Anthem to resolve this action instead of the normal dice pool, but you can’t get more successes than the action you mimic.
