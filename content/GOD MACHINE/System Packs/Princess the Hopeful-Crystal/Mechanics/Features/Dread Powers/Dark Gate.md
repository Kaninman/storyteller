The Dark World hangs off of our own, festering, waiting for a shaft of Light to stir up its awareness. To most of humanity, it’s a horrible threat. To Defilers and Darkspawn, it is home. This Horror can turn a Bleed into a Dark Gate with an extended action requiring five successes at an interval of 3 hours. They can open or close a Dark Gate by spending a Willpower, and while in the Dark World, they regain 1 extra Willpower for resting, or for indulging their Vice within the past 24 hours.
