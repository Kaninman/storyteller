The soul-sapping power of Darkness results in an embarrassment of riches for those who use people’s drained energy for dark ends. This Dread Power allows the Horror to store Willpower, no matter its source, into an energy matrix to be drawn out later. 

The Horror gains an object of Size 1-5 to serve as its energy storage. While touching the object, it may transfer Willpower to or from it; the object has a capacity for Willpower equal to 3 times its Size. The Horror can store Willpower it gains normally in this manner, and if it has the ability to gain Willpower from others, Willpower in excess of what it can gain is automatically transferred to the storage. A Horror with Fractured: Insecure can drain Willpower from others into the storage, and can use Willpower directly from it while touching it, but can not withdraw Willpower to use later. 

This Dread Power usually doesn’t count against a Horror’s maximum Dread Powers
