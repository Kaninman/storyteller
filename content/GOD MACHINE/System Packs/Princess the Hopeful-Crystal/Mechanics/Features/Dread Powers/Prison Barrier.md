The Horror can manifest a barrier to entrap or exclude others. Whether a wall of sickly green light, a grasping hand of obsidian, or a conjured sphere of ossuary bones, it’s able to prevent people from going where the Defiler doesn’t want them to go. 

Activating this ability costs 1 or more Willpower, contested by the target’s Dexterity + Athletics as they try to escape the forming barrier. Successful or not, the power creates an enclosed barrier a few meters in diameter around a point on the ground; success determines if the target is on the side the Horror wants them to be. The barrier has Durability equal to the Horror’s Potency and Structure equal to 2 times the Willpower spent on casting it. Once damaged sufficiently, the barrier shatters.

At 2 dots, the Horror can activate Dread Powers on anyone inside of its barriers as if it was touching that person, and the barrier forces a Clash of Wills for any other magic attempting to pierce it (such to locate or communicate with someone inside).
