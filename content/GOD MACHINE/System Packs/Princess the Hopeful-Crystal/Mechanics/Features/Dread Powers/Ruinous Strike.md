When this Horror attacks, it is as if the world attacks with it. The environment explodes with hostility. By spending a Willpower when attacking, the Horror can inflict a plausible environmental Tilt (kill the lights, rip open a cooling unit, cause a blizzard of debris, explode a water main), perform an attack from a source of environmental damage (such as fire or electricity), or completely rearrange terrain and cover in the immediate area.
