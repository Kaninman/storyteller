This Horror has a powered-up super form, accessible only when it invests significant energy. It spends 1 Willpower to activate Nightmare Trigger for the rest of the scene; while active, the Horror can spend multiple Willpower to add dice to a single roll. It always has to spend at least two Willpower per turn until either the scene ends, or it has less than two Willpower left.
