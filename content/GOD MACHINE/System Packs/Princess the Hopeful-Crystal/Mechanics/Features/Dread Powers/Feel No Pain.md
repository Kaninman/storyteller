Humanity lives in fear of pain. If you embrace the Darkness’s gifts, then that fear can be lifted, as long as you spread it to others. This Horror can spend a Willpower to turn off its sense of pain. It gains the Feel No Pain Tilt, granting it immunity to wound penalties and automatically succeeding any rolls to remain conscious. All sources of bashing damage, other than lethal or aggravated damage that was downgraded, is reduced to a single point. Actions involving physical might gain +3 bonus dice, but automatically incur one point of Bashing damage as the Horror breaks the limits of its body.
