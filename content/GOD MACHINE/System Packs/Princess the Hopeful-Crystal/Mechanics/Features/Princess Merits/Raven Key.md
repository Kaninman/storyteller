Your Phylactery becomes attuned with the Last City of Alhambra, allowing you to enter for respite. A Raven Key allows the bearer to open a Dark Gate to Alhambra without outside assistance, in a process that takes about two minutes. The bearer can take others with her, but only one at a time and they have to pass through security

Alhambra is not just an impenetrable, untraceable refuge that a Princess of Tears can use to recover, re-arm, and gather intelligence. The Raven Key is a gift that symbolizes the trust the Last City has placed in her, and the resolve they must have to see it protected. Bearers of a Raven Key may spend Willpower on Compromise rolls so long as the Compromise was done for something greater than personal benefit.

This Merit is granted freely at creation to Princesses of Tears. If the Princess is a current follower of the Queen of Tears, it is always active. Other Princesses can use this Merit if they have 3 or more dots of the Lacrima Signet; to actually make it through Alhambra’s gates they must be considered allies and pass a security screening every time
