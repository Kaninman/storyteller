Named more for that part in The Matrix than Alice in Wonderland, a White Rabbit is a clue from the Princess’s subconscious mind that leads her on a path into the Dreamlands, where some hint or clue to her predicament lies.

Once per chapter, a Princess with the White Rabbits Merit can seek out a vision in the Dreamlands. She has an opportunity to gather a Clue there tied to an ongoing mystery, or get a hint as to where she should start looking for answers. The Storyteller can also use this to provide plot hooks for the group, but this won’t consume the Princess’s one use per chapter.
