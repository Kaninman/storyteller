You’ve built up a fortress of delusions to stay in denial about what would destroy you. Instead of having a core personality that is naturally and vividly expressed, you distort yourself into caricature and reduce yourself to what you think that core personality should be, in the way that absolves you of guilt because this is what the True Heir is.

Any time you would lose Belief for any reason, you may instead take a randomly selected Fractured condition, all five of which inhibit your Willpower and interact with your Drive and Dread as the psychological strain of your denial increases. They are Fractured: *Avoidant, Fractured: Compulsive, Fractured: Insecure, Fractured: Obsessive, and Fractured: Unmoored. (p. 271)* Roll a die to determine which Condition you get, without eliminating any Conditions you already have. If the roll comes up a Condition you already have, you gain the Dissociated Condition (p. 268), which sends you into a fuguelike state where your mind rejects the messy reality, and substitutes the world in your head, where everything works exactly like you think it should.

All Fractured Conditions have the same resolution criteria. They can’t be resolved in any other way, unless the ability that granted them specifies it, and do not grant Beats. 

**Resolution:** Lose all Fractured Conditions when you regain a Belief by Putting It All On The Line. 

This Merit is granted freely at creation to Princesses of Mirrors. If the Princess is a current follower of the Queen of Mirrors, it is always active. Other Princesses can use this Merit if they have 3 or more dots of the Specchio Signet.
