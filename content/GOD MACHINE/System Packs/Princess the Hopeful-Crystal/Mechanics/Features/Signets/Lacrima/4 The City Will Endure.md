When you use the 2-dot ability of Lacrima to escape, you can retreat to the nearest Gate to Alhambra. You can take anything with you from the area you’re leaving: resources, equipment, personnel (who want to come along), anything you put work into or might need to reuse. If you know of a Darkspawn in or near the Dark World reflection of the area you escape to, you can summon it to take your place when you escape. Make sure to drop something that will be a problem for your pursuers, but you know they can defeat without too much collateral damage.
