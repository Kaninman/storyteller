Choose two Charms to become Fire-Attuned. Fire-Attuned Charms act through or on fire, heat, combustion, or illumination.

*Examples: A Step that propels with explosive force (but doesn’t let you make fine adjustments), a Kiai that wreaths your weapons in flame (but paints a target on you in ranged combat and can’t choose to deal bashing damage), or a Detect that reveals information by staring into flickering flames (but requires you to be in the dark to make them out).*
