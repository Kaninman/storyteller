Once per scene, you may spend a Willpower and name someone you truly love (not necessarily in a romantic sense). You are infused with their power in a blazing corona of passion. Each of your Attributes and Skills is raised to your beloved’s dots in that Attribute or Skill if it’s higher. You gain Wisps equal to their Integrity + Willpower. At the end of the scene, lose that same number of Wisps, if you still have any. You return to your mundane state and can’t Transform until you have a good night’s sleep. Love conquers all.
