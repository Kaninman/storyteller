You gain the “Blazing” Infusion Upgrade for two of your Charms:
“This Charm is infused with the power of Fuoco, granting it an ability related to fire, heat, combustion, or illumination.”

*Examples: Any Charm can radiate out a searing light. Flaming Buster and Kiai attacks start fires on anything they strike. A Force that manifests a tangible wall of heat like walking up to a blast furnace. A Step or Anthem where your footsteps / fire lyrics leave burning trails that can ignite anything. Detect that immolates a target to burn away all deception but leaves them unharmed. Restoration that turns an engulfing inferno into a source of warmth, cauterizes wounds for those you deem worthy, but still burns everyone and everything else.*
