Spend one Willpower and take one Resistant Lethal damage to activate this ability for the rest of the scene. While active, you can spend a Wisp on your turn to manifest an aura of pure destruction and corrosion, your hatred spilling out of the boundaries of your soul.

This aura extends out to a radius of 1 meter per Inner Light around you, and affects everything except yourself, the things you’re carrying or attending to, and the ground directly under your feet. Everything else in the radius takes one point of aggravated damage each turn the aura is active. This constant corrosion affects everything that enters the radius, which means bullets and other small projectiles disintegrate before they can even touch you; don’t bother rolling for them.

You cannot choose to spare allies this damage. If they’re in the aura, they take the damage.
