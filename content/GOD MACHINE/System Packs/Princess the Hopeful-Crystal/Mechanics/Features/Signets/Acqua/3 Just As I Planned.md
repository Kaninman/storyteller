Spend a Wisp and a Willpower and take an hour to research and prepare for an upcoming event, up to 1 day in the future per Inner Light you have. During that event, you can declare you have foreseen and planned for one obstacle it was possible for you to have turned up.

This can’t change things that have already happened—the Charm is a mechanical representation of things you have already done, not actually changing the past. If you trip an alarm, you can’t say “Actually, I didn’t trip that alarm,” but you could say “I cut the telephone line the alarm uses to call the security company when tripped, so it’s making a lot of noise in the building but nobody else knows yet.”
