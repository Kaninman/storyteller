While Transformed, you may spend one Wisp to become as omnipresent and ungraspable as the wind. Until the end of your next action, whenever your location is relevant to anything, you can choose to be in any location within 3 yards per dot of Inner Light you have, regardless of where you just were. You are considered to touch everything in that radius that you want to be touching, you can perform non-contested and non-resisted actions that would require you to run all over that area as instant actions, you can block multiple paths and see or attack from multiple angles. If there’s any point in that radius from which you could make an advantageous attack, you can make it from there; if there’s any point in that radius that is outside the range or visibility of counterattack you can be there when the opponent fires back. This can’t stop people from attacking you in melee as long as they can make it to your “real” location, and if you want to hide, you can’t act from any location someone you’re hiding from could see.
