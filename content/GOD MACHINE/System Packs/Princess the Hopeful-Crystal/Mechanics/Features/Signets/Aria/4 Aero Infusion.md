You gain the “Aero” Infusion Upgrade for two of your Charms:
“This Charm is infused with the power of Aria, granting it an ability related to air, wind, or sound.”

*Examples: A Plea or Anthem carried on the wind to any other outdoor location. A Buster or Force that captures all unattended objects in a whirlwind and drops them wherever you like. A Step that can interact with clouds or visible vapors as if they were solid objects, standing on or bouncing off of them. An Illusion that can create a perfect soundscape of gargantuan size and complexity.*
