This Nightmare is keyed to a specific individual, using it as an anchoring point, similar to Fetter; effects that require Fetter may also use Tether. It Tethers itself to an individual, but may move away from that person and use Dread Powers on anything. As long as its Tether is more concerned about the Nightmare than anything else, the Nightmare can remain Tethered indefinitely and send Essence back to the Nightscape remotely.

**Cause:** The Nightmare had the Tether Manifestation, which listed a prerequisite, and activated this Manifestation on someone who matched the prerequisite.

**Ending:** Undoing the prerequisite ends this Condition, reverts the Nightmare to Coalesced, and gives it the Undermined Nightmare Tilt.
