The dead can remain here without anguish or torture; dead souls exist as ghosts to get some extra time to wrap up what they were doing in life.

Ghosts in a Necropolis do not suffer from Essence Bleed, can use the Materialize Numen freely, and the entire Necropolis is considered to be an Anchor.

**Cause:** The Last City of Alhambra is a Necropolis. If there are others, they’re made by a different type of magic.
