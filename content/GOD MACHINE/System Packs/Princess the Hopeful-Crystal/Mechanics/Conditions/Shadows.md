Your Sensitivity to the Darkness wears on you, weakening your spirit. If you only have one stack of this Condition, you suffer only minor symptoms that are mostly psychosomatic. For each stack of this Condition after the first, your effective Inner Light is reduced by 1, to a minimum of 1, and you suffer increasing physical and emotional pain, sickness, disorientation, and anguish.

**Cause:** A nearby font of Pure Darkness, or proximity to repeated acts of great Darkness that have resisted efforts to stop them for too long.

**Beat:** Your miserable state causes you to miss or minimize an opportunity that would help your miserable state. 

**Resolution:** At the end of any scene in which you make significant progress against the source of Darkness, remove a stack. If you defeat the source of Darkness, remove all stacks.
