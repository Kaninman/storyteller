No. Not again. Never again. You failed someone, or failed yourself, in such a traumatic way that you’re now obsessed with never letting it happen again. If your character hasn’t taken action in the current scene to prevent this failure from happening again—even if there’s no way she could realistically do so—she gains a -2 penalty to all Mental and Social rolls.

**Cause:** You had a Dramatic Failure on a Compromise roll.

**Beat:** Your character’s obsession with this one source of danger or failure leads her to some other kind of danger or failure. 

**Resolution:** Gain a dot of Belief, lose another dot of Belief, or score an Exceptional Success on a Compromise roll.
