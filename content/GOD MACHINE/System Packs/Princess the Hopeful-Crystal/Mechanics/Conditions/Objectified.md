You’ve literally been objectified—you’ve been turned into a helpless object of Size 0-1! If you’re able to act at all, it’s in the very limited capacity of a doll or whatever form you were changed into. You lose 10-Again on all rolls, and 1s now subtract from your total successes. You also lose access to any supernatural abilities you had, lower your Speed by 75%, and reduce your max Health by 5.

**Cause:** You were subject to the Fell Collector Dread Power.

**Beat:** You manage to still participate in a scene; Objectified is so debilitating that anything you do is pretty much guaranteed to have been impeded by it.

**Ending:** The Horror that inflicted this Condition on you either agrees to release you, dies, or is beaten unconscious (sleeping normally doesn’t count). If the form you were forced into is unable to act at all, destroying it will free you instead of harming you.
