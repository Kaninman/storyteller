One of your Aspirations has been twisted by the voice of the Darkness. That Aspiration has been changed into a similar, but more destructive, version by the entity that inflicted this Condition.

**Cause:** You were subject to the Sinister Calling Dread Power and lost the contested roll.

**Beat:** You still get Beats for fulfilling that altered Aspiration, but only when fulfilling it is harmful to yourself or others.

**Ending:** Someone magically or nonmagically overcomes the effect by talking you through the distortions you’ve accepted, or you get away from the source of the Sinister Calling and then significantly harm yourself in pursuit of the twisted Aspiration.
