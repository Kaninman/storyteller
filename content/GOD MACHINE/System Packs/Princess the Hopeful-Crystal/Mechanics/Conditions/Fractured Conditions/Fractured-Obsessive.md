You know that what you want matters more than anything—than anyone—and that’s why you’re more important than everyone. That’s what makes it all justified.

You gain a new Dread of “Failing at any current Aspirations or Urges.” You do not gain Willpower from avoiding or conquering it, but it still counts for purposes of costing you Willpower to act against and effects that penalize you by keying into Dread.
